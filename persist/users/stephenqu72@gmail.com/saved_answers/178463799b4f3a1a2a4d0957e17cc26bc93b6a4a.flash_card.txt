### Front
What physical principle underpins Kepler’s Second Law, and what does the law describe?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Conservation of angular momentum.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $\frac{dA}{dt} = \frac{L}{2m} = \text{constant}$. No fixed constant.
- **When to use it:** To explain why a planet moves faster at perihelion (closer to the Sun) and slower at aphelion.
- **Common trap:** Confusing it with Kepler’s Third Law ($T^2 \propto r^3$) or total orbital energy conservation.
