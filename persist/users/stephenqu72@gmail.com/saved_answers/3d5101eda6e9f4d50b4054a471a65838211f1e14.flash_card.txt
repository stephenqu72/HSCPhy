### Front
How do you calculate the separation distance ($d$) required to suspend a point charge ($q$) of mass ($m$) between parallel plates with potential difference ($V$)?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Equilibrium is achieved when the upward electric force equals the downward gravitational force ($F_E = F_g$).
- **Formula:** $d = \frac{qV}{mg}$
- **When to use it:** When a charged particle is stationary (suspended) in a uniform electric field.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to use the magnitude of the charge or failing to convert units (e.g., mm to m) before calculating.
