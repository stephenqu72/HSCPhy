### Front
Which of the four fundamental forces is NOT described by the Standard Model of particle physics?

### Back
- Key idea: The Standard Model only covers electromagnetic, strong nuclear, and weak nuclear forces.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying the limitations of the Standard Model in physics/astrophysics questions.
- Common trap: Assuming gravity is included because it is a "fundamental force"; it is currently described by General Relativity, not quantum field theory.
