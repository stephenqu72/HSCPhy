### How to solve this type of question:
1.  **Identify the motion type:** Recognize that this is a classic "projectile motion" scenario. 
2.  **Recall the kinematics:** In projectile motion, horizontal acceleration is zero, and vertical acceleration is constant ($g$).
3.  **Evaluate vectors:** Remember that velocity is a vector. Since the vertical component of velocity increases over time due to gravity while the horizontal component stays the same, the resultant magnitude of the velocity vector must increase as the ball descends.
