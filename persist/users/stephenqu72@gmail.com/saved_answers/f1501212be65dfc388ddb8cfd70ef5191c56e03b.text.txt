$$\Delta E = hf = Rhc \left( \frac{1}{n_{lower}^2} - \frac{1}{n_{upper}^2} \right)$$

$$f = R_f \left( \frac{1}{n_{lower}^2} - \frac{1}{n_{upper}^2} \right)$$
where $R_f \approx 3.29 \times 10^{15} \text{ Hz}$ is the Rydberg constant in terms of frequency.
