### Front
How does mass affect the horizontal range of a projectile (ignoring air resistance)?

### Back
- Key idea: Projectile motion is independent of mass; all objects accelerate at the same rate ($g$) regardless of their weight.
- Formula / law / constant: $y = \frac{1}{2}gt^2$ and $x = u_x t$
- When to use it: To determine how changing the mass of an object affects its trajectory or time of flight.
- Common trap: Assuming heavier objects fall faster or travel differently; the time of flight is determined solely by vertical displacement and gravity.
