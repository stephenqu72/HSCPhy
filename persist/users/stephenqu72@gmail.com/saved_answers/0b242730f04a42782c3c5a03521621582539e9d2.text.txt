### Answer
The device shown is an **electrical transformer**. It works based on the principles of electromagnetic induction. 

1.  **Primary Coil:** An alternating current (AC) flowing through the primary coil creates a constantly changing magnetic flux within the soft iron core.
2.  **Magnetic Core:** The iron core serves to concentrate and guide this changing magnetic flux to the secondary coil.
3.  **Secondary Coil:** According to Faraday's Law of Induction, the changing magnetic flux passing through the secondary coil induces an electromotive force (EMF) or voltage across it. This allows for the stepping up or stepping down of voltage depending on the ratio of turns in the coils.

3.  **The Effect (1 mark):** Explain the output using Faraday’s Law. Link the changing magnetic flux to the induction of voltage in the secondary coil via electromagnetic induction. Mentioning the soft iron core as a medium to transfer the magnetic flux is also good practice to ensure the answer is technically robust.
