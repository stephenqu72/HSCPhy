### Front
How do you determine the direction of the magnetic field inside and around a solenoid?

### Back
- Key idea: The magnetic field lines inside a solenoid point from the South pole to the North pole.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Right-Hand Grip Rule (fingers = current direction; thumb = North pole/magnetic field direction).
- When to use it: Whenever determining the polarity or field direction of a current-carrying coil.
- Common trap: Using your left hand instead of your right, or confusing the field direction inside the solenoid (S to N) with the field direction outside (N to S).
