### Front
What is the fundamental strategy for solving projectile motion problems in HSC Physics?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Treat horizontal ($x$) and vertical ($y$) motion components as independent.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $a_x = 0$ and $a_y = -9.8 \text{ m s}^{-2}$.
- **When to use it:** For any motion involving an object launched into the air (ignoring air resistance).
- **Common trap:** Applying the same equations to both axes; horizontal velocity remains constant, while vertical velocity changes due to gravity.
