### Front
How does the acceleration-time graph of a projectile appear when the upward direction is defined as positive?

### Back
- Key idea: Acceleration is constant and directed downwards due to gravity.
- Formula / law / constant: $a = -g \approx -9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$
- When to use it: Any projectile motion problem neglecting air resistance.
- Common trap: Assuming acceleration is zero at the peak of the trajectory (velocity is zero, but acceleration remains $g$).
