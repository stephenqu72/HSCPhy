### Front
What are the two primary categories of elementary particles in the Standard Model, and how are they defined by their quantum spin?

### Back
- Key idea: Fermions (matter) and Bosons (force carriers).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Fermions: half-integer spin; Bosons: integer spin.
- When to use it: When classifying subatomic particles based on their physical function or properties.
- Common trap: Confusing composite particles (e.g., mesons/baryons) with elementary particles.
