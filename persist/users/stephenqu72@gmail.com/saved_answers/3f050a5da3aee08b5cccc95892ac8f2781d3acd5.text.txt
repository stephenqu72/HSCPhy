    $$E_{photon} = E_{high} - E_{low}$$
    Since the energy of a photon is inversely proportional to its wavelength ($\lambda$) according to the equation $E = \frac{hc}{\lambda}$, a larger energy gap results in a shorter wavelength (appearing on the left of the spectrum), while a smaller energy gap results in a longer wavelength (appearing on the right).
