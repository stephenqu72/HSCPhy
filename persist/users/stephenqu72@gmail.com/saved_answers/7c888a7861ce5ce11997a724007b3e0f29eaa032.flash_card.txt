### Front
How is acceleration related to net force and mass according to Newton's Second Law?

### Back
- Key idea: Acceleration is directly proportional to the net force acting on an object and inversely proportional to its mass.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $F_{net} = ma$
- When to use it: To calculate acceleration, mass, or resultant force when an object's motion changes.
- Common trap: Using a single applied force instead of the **net** (resultant) force.
