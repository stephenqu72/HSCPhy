### Front
What defines an inertial frame of reference?

### Back
- Key idea: A coordinate system that is not accelerating (velocity remains constant).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Newton’s First Law ($a = 0$).
- When to use it: To determine if Newtonian mechanics can be applied without the need for fictitious forces.
- Common trap: Assuming "constant speed" is sufficient; you must also ensure there is no change in **direction** (which would imply centripetal acceleration).
