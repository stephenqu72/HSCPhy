    $$m_{total} = 2 \times m_e \approx 2 \times 9.109 \times 10^{-31} \text{ kg} = 1.8218 \times 10^{-30} \text{ kg}$$
3.  **Use Rest Mass Energy:** In high-energy physics, it is standard knowledge that the rest mass energy of a single electron is approximately $0.511 \text{ MeV}$.

    $$E_{total} = E_{electron} + E_{positron} = 0.511 \text{ MeV} + 0.511 \text{ MeV} = 1.022 \text{ MeV}$$
