To solve this problem, we use the formula for torque:
$$\tau = F \times d = m \times g \times d$$
Since $g$ (acceleration due to gravity) is constant for all diagrams, we can compare the net torque by calculating the sum of the moments ($m \times d$) for each side of the fulcrum and finding the difference between them. Let $d$ be the unit distance from the fulcrum.
