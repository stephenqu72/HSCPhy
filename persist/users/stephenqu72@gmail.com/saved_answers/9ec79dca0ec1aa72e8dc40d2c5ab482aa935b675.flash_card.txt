### Front
What are the two primary pieces of evidence supporting the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- Key idea: 
    1. **Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB):** Residual, uniform "afterglow" radiation proving the early universe was hot and dense.
    2. **Hubble’s Law/Redshift:** Galaxies are receding from us; this expansion implies a singular point of origin.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $v = H_0 d$ (Hubble’s Law)
- When to use it: Any question asking for observational evidence of the Big Bang or the evolution of the universe.
- Common trap: Describing the Big Bang event itself rather than focusing on the *evidence* used to verify it.
