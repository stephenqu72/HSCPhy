### Front
How does Einstein’s mass-energy equivalence support the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- Key idea: Energy in the early, high-temperature universe converted into matter via pair production as the universe cooled.
- Formula / law: $E = mc^2$
- When to use it: To explain the origin of matter (protons, neutrons, electrons) from the initial state of pure radiation.
- Common trap: Stating that matter was "created from nothing" rather than being converted from pre-existing high-energy radiation.
