### Front
In the context of the Standard Model, what is the classification of an electron?

### Back
- Key idea: Electrons are fundamental particles categorized as leptons.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying the composition of matter; electrons have no internal structure and are not made of quarks.
- Common trap: Assuming electrons are hadrons or composed of quarks (which only applies to baryons/mesons).
