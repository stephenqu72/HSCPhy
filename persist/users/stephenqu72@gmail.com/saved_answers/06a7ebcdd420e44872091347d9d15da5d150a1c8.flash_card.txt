### Front
What evidence did Edwin Hubble use to conclude that the universe is expanding?

### Back
- Key idea: Systematic redshift of distant galaxies indicates they are receding from us.
- Formula / law: Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0d$)
- When to use it: To explain the observational basis for the Big Bang theory or universal expansion.
- Common trap: Confusing Hubble's discovery of the *expansion* (redshift) with his earlier discovery of the *scale* of the universe (using Cepheid variables).
