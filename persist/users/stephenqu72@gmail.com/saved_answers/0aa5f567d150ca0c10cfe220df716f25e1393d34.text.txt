*   Energy is **released** in a nuclear reaction if the products have a higher average binding energy per nucleon than the reactants (moving "up" the curve toward the peak, typically around Iron-56).
*   Energy is **absorbed** if the product has a lower average binding energy per nucleon than the reactants (moving "down" the curve).
