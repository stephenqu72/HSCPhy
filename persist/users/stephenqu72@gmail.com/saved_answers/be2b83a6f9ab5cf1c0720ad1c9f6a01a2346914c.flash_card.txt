### Front
How do you identify experimental variables in a circular motion investigation involving mass and period?

### Back
- Key idea: Identify the variable manipulated (independent), the variable measured (dependent), and variables kept constant (controlled).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: Whenever an experimental procedure is described to ensure a fair test.
- Common trap: Mistaking the controlled variables (e.g., radius, hanging mass) for the independent variable being tested.
