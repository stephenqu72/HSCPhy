### Front
What is the relationship between the kinetic energy ($K$) and gravitational potential energy ($U$) of a satellite in a circular orbit?

### Back
- Key idea: The Virial Theorem dictates that for a circular orbit, $K = -1/2 U$.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $U = -\frac{GMm}{r}$ and $K = \frac{GMm}{2r}$.
- When to use it: To determine energy values at specific orbital radii ($r$) or to sketch energy vs. radius graphs.
- Common trap: Forgetting that $U$ is always negative; $E_{total} = K + U = 1/2 U = -K$.
