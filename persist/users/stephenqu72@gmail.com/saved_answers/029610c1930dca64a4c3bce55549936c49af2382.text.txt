### Explanation:
In particle physics, particles that "occupy space" are generally categorized as matter particles (fermions like quarks and leptons), while force carriers (bosons) are considered point-like particles that mediate interactions. The Z boson is a gauge boson responsible for the weak nuclear interaction. Unlike the W boson, which carries an electric charge of $\pm 1$, the Z boson is electrically neutral ($0$).
