### Front
What is the formula for the banking angle ($\theta$) of a frictionless track required to maintain a circular path at speed $v$ and radius $r$?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The horizontal component of the normal force provides the centripetal force, while the vertical component balances weight.
- **Formula:** $\tan\theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$
- **When to use it:** Solving for the ideal banking angle of a circular path where no friction is present.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to convert speed from km/h to m/s before calculation.
