### Front
How does launch height ($h > 0$) affect the optimal angle for maximum range compared to the standard $45^\circ$ assumption?

### Back
- Key idea: The optimal launch angle for maximum range is less than $45^\circ$ when the release height is above the landing surface.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Projectile kinematics ($y = y_0 + v_y t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2$).
- When to use it: Whenever a projectile is launched from an elevated position (e.g., shot put, high-cliff diving).
- Common trap: Assuming $45^\circ$ is always optimal; this only applies when launch and landing heights are equal.
