### Front
Identify the Standard Model boson that mediates the weak nuclear force and has zero electric charge.

### Back
- Key idea: The Z boson is a neutral gauge boson that mediates the weak nuclear interaction.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When classifying subatomic particles based on their force-carrying properties and electric charge.
- Common trap: Confusing the Z boson (neutral) with the W boson (charged $\pm 1e$), which also mediates the weak nuclear force.
