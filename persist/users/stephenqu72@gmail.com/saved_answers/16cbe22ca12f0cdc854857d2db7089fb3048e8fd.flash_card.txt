### Front
State Kepler’s First Law of Planetary Motion.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Planets follow elliptical orbits with the Sun at one focus.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Kepler’s First Law; Ellipse geometry.
- **When to use it:** When asked to describe orbital paths or identify planetary motion principles.
- **Common trap:** Confusing Kepler’s First Law with Newton’s Law of Universal Gravitation ($F = G\frac{m_1m_2}{r^2}$).
