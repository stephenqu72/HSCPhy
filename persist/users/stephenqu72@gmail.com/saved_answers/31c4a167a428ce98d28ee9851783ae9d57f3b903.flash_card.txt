### Front
Compare the operational principles of a magnetic loudspeaker and an induction motor.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Magnetic loudspeakers operate via the **Motor Effect** (Lorentz force); induction motors operate via **Electromagnetic Induction** (Faraday’s/Lenz’s Laws).
- **Formula / law:** Loudspeaker: $F = BIL\sin\theta$; Induction Motor: $\varepsilon = -n\frac{\Delta\Phi}{\Delta t}$ (induced eddy currents).
- **When to use it:** When asked to contrast energy conversion or operating mechanisms between devices.
- **Common trap:** Confusing the Motor Effect (direct current interaction) with Electromagnetic Induction (induced current interaction).
