To solve this question, we must first recall the fundamental difference between scalar and vector quantities in physics:

*   **Scalar quantities** have only magnitude (size). Examples include: time, distance, speed, mass, and energy.
*   **Vector quantities** have both magnitude and direction. Examples include: displacement, velocity, acceleration, and force.
