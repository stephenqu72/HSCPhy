### Front
How do you determine the direction of the net electrostatic force on a point charge in a system of multiple charges?

### Back
- Key idea: Perform vector addition of all individual electrostatic forces acting on the point charge.
- Formula / law: Coulomb’s Law ($F = k\frac{|q_1q_2|}{r^2}$) and the Law of Electrostatics (like charges repel, opposites attract).
- When to use it: Whenever multiple source charges exert forces on a single target charge.
- Common trap: Neglecting the vector nature of force; always resolve forces into horizontal and vertical components if they are not collinear.
