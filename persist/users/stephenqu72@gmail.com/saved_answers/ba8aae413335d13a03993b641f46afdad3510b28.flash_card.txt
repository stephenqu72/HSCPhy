### Front
What is the relationship between the total mechanical energy ($E_T$) of a satellite and its orbital radius ($r$)?

### Back
- Key idea: $E_T$ is always negative (bound state) and increases toward zero as $r$ increases.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $E_T = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- When to use it: To determine orbital energy changes or identify the correct $E$ vs $r$ graph for stable circular orbits.
- Common trap: Thinking that "increasing energy" means the value becomes more negative; remember that moving from e.g., $-10$ to $-5$ is a mathematical increase.
