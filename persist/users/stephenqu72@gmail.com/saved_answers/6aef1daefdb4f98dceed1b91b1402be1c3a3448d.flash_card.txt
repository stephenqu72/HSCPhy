### Front
What is the reaction force pair to the gravitational force exerted by the Earth on a person?

### Back
- Key idea: Newton’s Third Law—forces exist in equal and opposite pairs acting on *different* objects.
- Law: Newton’s Third Law ($F_{A \text{ on } B} = -F_{B \text{ on } A}$).
- When to use it: To identify interaction pairs; the reaction to the Earth’s gravitational pull on an object is that object pulling the Earth upwards with equal force.
- Common trap: Confusing the "normal force" (from the floor) with the "gravitational reaction force" (from the Earth).
