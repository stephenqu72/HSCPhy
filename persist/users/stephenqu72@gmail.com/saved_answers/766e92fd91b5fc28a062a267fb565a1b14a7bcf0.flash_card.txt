### Front
How does a rider increase the amplitude of a swing by shifting their body position?

### Back
- Key idea: Work done by the rider raises their centre of mass (CM) at the highest point, increasing gravitational potential energy which converts to kinetic energy during the descent.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $W = \Delta GPE = mg\Delta h$
- When to use it: Any system involving driven oscillations or changing the CM of a pendulum to increase mechanical energy.
- Common trap: Attributing the speed increase to centripetal force or pulling on the ropes, rather than the work done against gravity by shifting the CM.
