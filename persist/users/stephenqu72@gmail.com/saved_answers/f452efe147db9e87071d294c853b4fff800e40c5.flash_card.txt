### Front
What is the launch angle for maximum range of a projectile on level ground, and why is it independent of gravity ($g$)?

### Back
- Key idea: Maximum range occurs when $\sin(2\theta)$ is maximized. Since $\sin(90^\circ)=1$, the optimal angle is $45^\circ$.
- Formula / law: $R = \frac{v^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$
- When to use it: Projectile motion problems on level ground where air resistance is ignored.
- Common trap: Assuming the $45^\circ$ rule applies when the landing point is higher or lower than the launch point (it does not).
