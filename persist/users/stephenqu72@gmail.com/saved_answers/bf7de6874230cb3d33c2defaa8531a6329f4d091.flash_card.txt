### Front
What is the physical condition required for resonance to occur in a system?

### Back
- Key idea: Resonance occurs when an external driving force matches the system's natural frequency, resulting in maximum amplitude.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $f_{driving} = f_{natural}$
- When to use it: Any scenario involving standing waves, oscillations, or vibrations (e.g., resonance tubes, bridge collapses, musical instruments).
- Common trap: Confusing "maximum amplitude" with "maximum frequency"; resonance refers to the *transfer* of energy at a specific matching frequency.
