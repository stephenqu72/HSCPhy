### Front
Why do physicists measure multiple oscillations rather than a single one when determining the period of a pendulum?

### Back
- Key idea: Reduces the relative uncertainty caused by human reaction time.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: Whenever a measurement involves a manual stop-watch timing process.
- Common trap: Thinking it reduces systematic errors (like friction or air resistance); it only addresses random uncertainty.
