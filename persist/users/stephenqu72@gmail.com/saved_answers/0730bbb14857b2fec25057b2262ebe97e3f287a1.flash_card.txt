### Front
How do you calculate the torque ($\tau$) exerted by an object on a hinged shelf?

### Back
- Key idea: Torque is the product of the force (weight) and the perpendicular distance from the pivot.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\tau = F_{\perp}d$ where $F = mg$ ($g = 9.8\text{ m/s}^2$).
- When to use it: Whenever determining the rotational effect of a force about a pivot point in equilibrium problems.
- Common trap: Forgetting to convert mass units to kilograms ($g \rightarrow kg$) or failing to measure distance from the correct pivot point.
