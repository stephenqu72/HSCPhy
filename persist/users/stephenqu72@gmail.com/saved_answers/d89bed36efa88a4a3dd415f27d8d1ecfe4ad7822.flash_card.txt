### Front
Identify the "odd one out" among these four: Gravity, Electrostatic charge, Weak nuclear force, and Strong nuclear force. Why?

### Back
- Key idea: Only three are fundamental forces; one is a property of matter.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When classifying forces in the Standard Model.
- Common trap: Confusing the property (electrostatic charge) with the fundamental force itself (electromagnetic force).
