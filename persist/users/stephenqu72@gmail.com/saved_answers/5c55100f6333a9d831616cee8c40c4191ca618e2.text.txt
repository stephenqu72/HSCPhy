**Explanation:**
Looking at the diagram for Step 1, two protons ($^1_1H$) combine to form one deuterium nucleus ($^2_1H$), one positron ($^0_{+1}e$), and one neutrino ($\nu_e$). To balance a nuclear equation, the sum of the atomic numbers (bottom) and the sum of the mass numbers (top) must be equal on both sides of the equation.

*   Mass numbers: $1 + 1 = 2 + 0$ (Balanced)
*   Atomic numbers: $1 + 1 = 1 + 1$ (Balanced)

    $$E = 0.0009992 \, u \times 931.5 \, \text{MeV}/u \approx 0.93175 \, \text{MeV}$$
