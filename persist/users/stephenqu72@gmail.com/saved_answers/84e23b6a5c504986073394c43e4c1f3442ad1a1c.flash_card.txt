### Front
What is Hubble’s Law and what does it indicate about the universe?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Galaxies move away from us at a velocity proportional to their distance, providing evidence that space-time itself is expanding.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $v = H_0d$ (where $H_0$ is the Hubble constant).
- **When to use it:** When explaining the expansion of the universe or calculating the recession velocity of distant galaxies based on their distance.
- **Common trap:** Confusing redshift with the Doppler effect of sound; remember that redshift is due to the stretching of light waves as space expands, not just motion through space.
