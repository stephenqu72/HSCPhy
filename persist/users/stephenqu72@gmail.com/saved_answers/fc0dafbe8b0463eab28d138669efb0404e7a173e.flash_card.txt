### Front
What physics principle is demonstrated by "Newton’s Cannonball," and how does it relate to orbital motion?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Orbits are simply projectile motion where the object's horizontal velocity is high enough that the Earth's surface curves away at the same rate the object falls.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Newton’s Cannonball diagram (horizontal velocity vs. gravitational curvature).
- **When to use it:** To explain the transition from terrestrial projectile motion to circular/elliptical orbital motion and satellite motion.
- **Common trap:** Confusing this with Kepler’s Laws; Kepler’s Laws describe the *geometry* of orbits, whereas Newton’s Cannonball explains the *physical mechanism* of how an object enters orbit.
