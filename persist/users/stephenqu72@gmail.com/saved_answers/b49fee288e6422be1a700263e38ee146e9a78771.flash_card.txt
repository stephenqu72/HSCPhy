### Front
How does a rider increase the mechanical energy of a swing?

### Back
- Key idea: The rider performs internal work to raise their centre of mass (COM) relative to the pivot, increasing the system's gravitational potential energy.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Conservation of Energy ($E_{total} = PE_g + KE$); $PE_g = mgh$.
- When to use it: Any problem involving oscillating systems where external work is performed to increase amplitude.
- Common trap: Thinking movement alone creates energy; the energy comes specifically from the work done to raise the COM against gravity.
