### Front
What are the primary variables in a pendulum experiment investigating the relationship between length and period?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Independent (manipulated), Dependent (measured), and Controlled (constant) variables.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}$
- **When to use it:** To ensure experimental validity; length is the independent variable, period is the dependent variable, and mass/amplitude are controlled.
- **Common trap:** Confusing amplitude as a dependent variable; for small angles, the period is independent of amplitude (isochronism).
