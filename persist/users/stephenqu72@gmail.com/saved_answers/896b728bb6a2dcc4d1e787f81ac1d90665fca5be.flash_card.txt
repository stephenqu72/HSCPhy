### Front
Define 'redshift' and identify the scientist who used it as evidence for the Big Bang Theory.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Light from a receding source is stretched to longer (redder) wavelengths due to the Doppler effect or the expansion of space.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Hubble's Law ($v = H_0d$). Scientist: Edwin Hubble.
- **When to use it:** When analyzing the motion of galaxies or providing evidence for an expanding universe.
- **Common trap:** Confusing Doppler redshift (relative motion through space) with Cosmological redshift (stretching of space itself). Both lead to the same observed effect.
