### Front
What physical process is responsible for the phenomenon of acoustic resonance in pipes?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The superposition of incident and reflected waves forming a standing wave pattern.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** When explaining how musical instruments or air columns produce specific pitches based on their length and boundary conditions.
- **Common trap:** Assuming resonance happens at *any* frequency; it only occurs when the source frequency matches the system's natural resonant frequencies.
