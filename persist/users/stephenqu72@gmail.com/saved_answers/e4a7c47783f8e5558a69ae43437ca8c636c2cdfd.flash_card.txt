### Front
How do you structure a 9-mark "justify" response for Module 8 (From the Universe to the Atom) concerning experimental evidence for particle physics?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Use a thematic progression linking specific experimental interactions (fields/collisions) to their respective paradigm shifts in atomic and particle theory.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** When asked to justify how experimental observations led to the current understanding of atomic structure or the Standard Model.
- **Common trap:** Listing historical facts chronologically without explicitly stating the *link* between the experimental observation (e.g., deflection in a field) and the theoretical conclusion (e.g., charge quantization or nuclear existence).
