### Question 9: Calculation
At the lowest point (Z) of a vertical circle, the forces acting on the ball are the tension ($T$) acting upwards and the weight ($mg$) acting downwards. The net force provides the centripetal force ($F_c = \frac{mv^2}{r} = mr\omega^2$):

$$T - mg = \frac{mv^2}{r} \implies T = m(g + r\omega^2)$$

**1. Identify given values:**
*   $m = 12\text{ kg}$
*   $r = 2.3\text{ m}$
*   Frequency ($f$) = $3\text{ rev/s}$
*   $g \approx 9.8\text{ m/s}^2$

**2. Calculate angular velocity ($\omega$):**
$$\omega = 2\pi f = 2 \times \pi \times 3 = 6\pi \approx 18.85\text{ rad/s}$$

**3. Calculate tension ($T$):**
$$T = 12 \times (9.8 + 2.3 \times (6\pi)^2)$$
$$T = 12 \times (9.8 + 2.3 \times 355.3)$$
$$T = 12 \times (9.8 + 817.2) = 12 \times 827 \approx 9924\text{ N}$$
Converted to kN, this is approximately $9.9\text{ kN}$.

**Correct Answer: D**

### Question 10: Conceptual Analysis
To prevent the rope from breaking, we must decrease the tension ($T$). As derived above, the tension at the bottom is:
$$T = mg + mr\omega^2 = mg + mr(2\pi f)^2 = mg + 4\pi^2 mrf^2$$

To reduce $T$:
*   **A (Increase mass):** Increases $T$. (Incorrect)
*   **B (Increase radius):** Increases $T$ because $r$ is in the numerator. (Incorrect)
*   **C (Decrease radius):** Decreases $T$ because $T$ is directly proportional to $r$. (Correct)
*   **D (Increase rate of rotation):** Increases $T$ because $T$ is proportional to the square of the frequency. (Incorrect)

**Correct Answer: C**
