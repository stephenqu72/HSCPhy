### 1. Diagram Requirements
In the box provided, you should draw:

### 2. Explanation of Analysis
Rutherford’s analysis of the Geiger-Marsden results destroyed the "Plum Pudding" model of the atom. Here is the logical flow for your 6-mark response:

*   **Observation 1:** Most alpha particles passed through the gold foil undeflected.
    *   **Inference:** The atom is composed mostly of empty space.
*   **Observation 2:** A small number of alpha particles were deflected at large angles.
    *   **Inference:** There is a concentrated, positively charged region at the center of the atom (the nucleus). Since like charges repel, the positive alpha particles were repelled by this dense positive core.
*   **Observation 3:** An extremely small fraction of alpha particles were reflected directly backward.
    *   **Inference:** The mass of the atom is concentrated in this tiny, dense central nucleus, rather than being distributed throughout the volume of the atom as J.J. Thomson’s model suggested.

**Summary of Advancement:**
Rutherford proposed the **Nuclear Model of the Atom**: the atom consists of a very small, dense, positively charged nucleus at the center, orbited by electrons at a large distance, explaining why the majority of the atom is empty space.
