To solve this problem, we need to consider the dynamics of circular motion:

1.  **Identify the required Net Force:** For an object to move in a uniform horizontal circle, there must be a net force acting on it known as the **centripetal force**. By definition, the centripetal force must be directed toward the center of the circular path. Since the track is horizontal and circular, the center of the circle lies along the horizontal radius of the track. Therefore, the net force must be directed **horizontally toward the center of the track**.
2.  **Analyze the Forces:**

    $$\vec{F}_{net} = \vec{F}_g + \vec{F}_N$$
