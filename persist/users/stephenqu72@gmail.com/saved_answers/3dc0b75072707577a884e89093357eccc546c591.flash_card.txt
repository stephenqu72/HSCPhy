### Front
How do you calculate the horizontal landing position of a projectile on level ground?

### Back
- Key idea: Horizontal range ($R$) depends on initial velocity, launch angle, and gravity.
- Formula: $R = \frac{u^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$
- When to use it: Projectile motion problems where the launch and landing points are at the same vertical elevation.
- Common trap: Forgetting to double the angle (using $\sin \theta$ instead of $\sin 2\theta$) or using degrees when the calculator is set to radians.
