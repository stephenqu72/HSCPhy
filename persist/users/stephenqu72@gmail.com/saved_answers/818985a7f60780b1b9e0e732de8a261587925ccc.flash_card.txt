### Front
How does Hubble’s Law provide evidence for the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The observed linear relationship ($v = H_0D$) shows the universe is expanding; extrapolating this backward suggests a singular, dense origin.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Hubble's Law: $v = H_0D$ (where $H_0$ is the Hubble constant).
- **When to use it:** When explaining the expansion of the universe or evidence for cosmological theories.
- **Common trap:** Confusing redshift with the "Doppler effect" of sound; in cosmology, redshift is due to the expansion of space itself, not just the motion of objects through space.
