### Front
How do you determine the correct precision (decimal places) when **adding or subtracting** measured values?

### Back
- Key idea: The final result must be rounded to the same precision (the least number of decimal places) as the least precise measurement.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When calculating sums, differences, or perimeters from measurements with different levels of uncertainty.
- Common trap: Over-precision (rounding to too many decimal places) or forgetting to standardize units before performing the calculation.
