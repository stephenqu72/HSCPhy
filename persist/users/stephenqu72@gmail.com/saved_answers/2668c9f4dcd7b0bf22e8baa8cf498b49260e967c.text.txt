### 2. Calculate the Decay Constant ($\lambda$)
The relationship between the decay constant ($\lambda$) and the half-life ($t_{1/2}$) is given by the formula:
$$\lambda = \frac{\ln(2)}{t_{1/2}}$$

$$\lambda = \frac{0.6931}{13}$$
$$\lambda \approx 0.0533\text{ s}^{-1}$$
