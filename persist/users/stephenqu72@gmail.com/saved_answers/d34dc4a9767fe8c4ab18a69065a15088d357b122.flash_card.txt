### Front
How do you determine the polarity of point charges using an electric field line diagram?

### Back
- Key idea: Field lines originate from positive charges and terminate at negative charges.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Directional arrows: Away (+) / Toward (-).
- When to use it: To identify charge sign in electrostatic field diagrams.
- Common trap: Assuming arrows pointing toward each other imply opposite charges; always check the specific direction of individual field lines.
