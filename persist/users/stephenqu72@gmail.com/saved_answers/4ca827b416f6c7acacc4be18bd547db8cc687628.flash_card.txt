### Front
For a projectile launched at $45^\circ$ on level ground, what is the relationship between its horizontal range ($R$) and maximum height ($H$)?

### Back
- Key idea: At $\theta = 45^\circ$, the horizontal range is four times the maximum height.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $R = 4H$
- When to use it: Use for rapid calculation or as a check in projectile motion questions involving a $45^\circ$ launch angle.
- Common trap: Forgetting that this specific $R=4H$ ratio **only** applies when $\theta = 45^\circ$; it is not a universal projectile relationship.
