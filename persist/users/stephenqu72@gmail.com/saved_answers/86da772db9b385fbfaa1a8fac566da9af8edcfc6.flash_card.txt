### Front
What conditions maximize torque ($\tau$) on a rigid object?

### Back
- Key idea: Maximize both the radial distance ($r$) from the pivot and the perpendicular component of the force.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\tau = rF\sin(\theta)$
- When to use it: Whenever a force is applied to a lever, beam, or rotating object.
- Common trap: Assuming torque is maximum when the force is largest, ignoring the angle ($\theta$) or the distance from the pivot ($r$).
