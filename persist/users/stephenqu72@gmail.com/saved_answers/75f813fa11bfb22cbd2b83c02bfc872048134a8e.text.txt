3.  **Gamma ($\gamma$) rays** are high-energy photons with very low ionizing power compared to particles.

Since alpha particles have significantly higher ionizing power than beta particles or gamma rays due to their larger mass and greater electric charge, the isotope that produces alpha radiation will have the highest ionizing capability.
