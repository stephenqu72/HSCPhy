To solve this question, we must understand the scientific definitions of **accuracy** and **reliability** (often referred to as **precision**) in the context of experimental data:

*   **Accuracy:** How close the measurements are to the "true" or expected value (the bullseye).
*   **Reliability (Precision):** How close the measurements are to each other (the grouping of the darts).
