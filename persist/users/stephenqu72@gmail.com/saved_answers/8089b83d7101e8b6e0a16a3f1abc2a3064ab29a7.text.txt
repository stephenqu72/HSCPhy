    $$\varepsilon = -N \frac{\Delta\Phi}{\Delta t}$$
2.  **Interpret the graph:** The magnitude of the induced voltage is greatest when the **rate of change** of the magnetic flux is at its maximum. On a graph of flux versus time, the rate of change of flux is represented by the **gradient (slope)** of the curve.
3.  **Analyze the points:**

4.  **Conclusion:** Because the magnitude of the rate of change of flux is greatest at the points where the graph crosses the horizontal axis, the magnitude of the induced voltage is greatest at **X and Z**.
