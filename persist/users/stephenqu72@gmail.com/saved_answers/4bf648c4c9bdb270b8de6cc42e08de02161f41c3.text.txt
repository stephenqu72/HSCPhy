### Answer
Erwin Schrödinger developed the wave mechanical model of the atom, which treats electrons as standing waves rather than discrete particles following defined orbits. By introducing the Schrödinger equation, he provided a mathematical method to determine the probability of finding an electron in a specific region of space, leading to the development of the electron cloud model and the concept of atomic orbitals.
