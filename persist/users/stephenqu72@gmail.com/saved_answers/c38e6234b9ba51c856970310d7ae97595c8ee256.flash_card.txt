### Front
Why should you measure the time for multiple oscillations (e.g., $n=5$) instead of a single period when conducting a pendulum experiment?

### Back
- Key idea: Reduction of absolute uncertainty caused by human reaction time.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\Delta T = \frac{\Delta t_{reaction}}{n}$
- When to use it: Whenever measuring time-based intervals (like oscillations) to improve experimental precision.
- Common trap: Thinking it eliminates systematic errors like air resistance; it only reduces random errors associated with timing.
