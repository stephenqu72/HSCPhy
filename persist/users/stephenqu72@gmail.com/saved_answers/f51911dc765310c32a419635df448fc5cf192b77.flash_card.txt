### Front
What is the minimum height ($h$) required for an object to successfully complete a vertical loop of radius ($r$) starting from rest?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Conservation of Mechanical Energy combined with the circular motion requirement $v^2 = gr$ at the top of the loop.
- **Formula:** $h = 2.5r$
- **When to use it:** When calculating the minimum release height to maintain contact with the track throughout a circular loop (where normal force at the top is effectively zero).
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to include the potential energy at the top of the loop ($PE = mg \times 2r$) or failing to cancel mass $m$ early in the calculation.
