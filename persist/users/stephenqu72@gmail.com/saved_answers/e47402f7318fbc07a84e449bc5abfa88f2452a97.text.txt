$$W = mg = 7800\text{ kg} \times 9.8\text{ m s}^{-2} = 76,440\text{ N}$$

$$F_{net} = \text{Thrust} - W = 90,000\text{ N} - 76,440\text{ N} = +13,560\text{ N}$$
Because the net force is positive, the rocket experiences an upward acceleration.
