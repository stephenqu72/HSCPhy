### Front
What is the formula for the normal reaction force ($F_N$) of an object at the bottom of a vertical circular path?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The normal force must provide both the weight support and the required centripetal force.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $F_N = F_c + mg$ (where $F_c = \frac{mv^2}{r}$)
- **When to use it:** When calculating forces on an object moving through the lowest point of a vertical circle.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to add the weight ($mg$); students often incorrectly assume $F_N = F_c$ or $F_N = mg$.
