### Front
In a uniform electric field, how do you determine the distance ($d$) between two parallel plates given a graph of Voltage ($V$) vs. Electric Field Strength ($E$)?

### Back
- Key idea: The slope of a $V$ vs. $E$ graph represents the distance between plates.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $V = E \times d$; slope $= \Delta V / \Delta E = d$.
- When to use it: When solving for plate separation in parallel plate capacitors or uniform field problems.
- Common trap: Forgetting to convert units (e.g., meters to centimeters) or confusing the axes (slope is $d$, not $1/d$).
