### How to solve this type of problem
*   **Identify the spectral type:** When looking at a spectrum, first distinguish between *continuous*, *absorption*, and *emission* spectra.
    *   *Continuous:* A smooth, unbroken rainbow (e.g., hot solids like a candle flame or light bulb).
    *   *Absorption:* A continuous spectrum with dark lines (e.g., light passing through a cool gas).
    *   *Emission:* Sharp, discrete lines on a dark background (e.g., gas discharge tubes, LED/Fluorescent lights).
