### Front
What are the energy relationships for a satellite in a stable circular orbit around a central mass $M$?

### Back
- Key idea: Total energy is the sum of kinetic and potential energy, governed by the Virial Theorem.
- Formula / law: 
  - $E_p = -2E_k$
  - $E_{total} = -E_k$
  - $E_{total} = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- When to use it: To calculate changes in satellite energy, orbital height, or compare energy types in circular orbits.
- Common trap: Forgetting the negative sign in gravitational potential energy ($E_p$), which results in a negative total mechanical energy.
