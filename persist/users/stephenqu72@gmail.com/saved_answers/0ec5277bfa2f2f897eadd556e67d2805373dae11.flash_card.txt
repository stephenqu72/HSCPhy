### Front
How does the angle of launch ($\theta$) affect the maximum height ($H$) of a projectile?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Maximum height is determined solely by the initial vertical velocity component ($u_y = u \sin \theta$).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $H = \frac{(u \sin \theta)^2}{2g}$
- **When to use it:** Use when asked to maximize height or compare trajectories for a given initial velocity.
- **Common trap:** Assuming horizontal velocity ($u_x$) contributes to height; it only influences the range and time of flight.
