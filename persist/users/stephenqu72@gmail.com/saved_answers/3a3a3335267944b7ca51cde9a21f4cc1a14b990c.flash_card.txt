### Front
Which experimental technique provided the primary evidence that protons and neutrons are composite particles?

### Back
- Key idea: High-energy lepton-nucleon scattering (Deep Inelastic Scattering) reveals internal structure by probing with short de Broglie wavelengths.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$ (De Broglie wavelength)
- When to use it: When asked how scientists experimentally determined that nucleons are not fundamental particles.
- Common trap: Confusing DIS with alpha particle scattering (Rutherford) or neutron discovery (Chadwick).
