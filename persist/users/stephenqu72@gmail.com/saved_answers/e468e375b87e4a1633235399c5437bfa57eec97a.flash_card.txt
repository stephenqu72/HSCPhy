### Front
Explain the role of fundamental forces in the formation of neutral helium-4 atoms during the Big Bang.

### Back
- Key idea: The Strong Nuclear Force binds protons and neutrons into the nucleus (nucleosynthesis), while the Electromagnetic Force allows the nucleus to capture electrons (recombination) to form neutral atoms.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: To answer questions regarding the Big Bang theory, cosmic evolution, or the formation of matter.
- Common trap: Confusing "nucleosynthesis" (forming nuclei at 20 mins) with "recombination" (forming neutral atoms at 240,000 years). Ensure you attribute the correct force to each stage.
