### Front
What is the formula for the total mechanical energy of a satellite in a stable circular orbit?

### Back
- Key idea: Total energy is the sum of kinetic ($E_k = \frac{GMm}{2r}$) and potential ($E_p = -\frac{GMm}{r}$) energy.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $E_{total} = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- When to use it: To calculate the energy required to place a satellite in orbit or change its orbital radius.
- Common trap: Forgetting the negative sign; the total energy is always negative, representing a bound system.
