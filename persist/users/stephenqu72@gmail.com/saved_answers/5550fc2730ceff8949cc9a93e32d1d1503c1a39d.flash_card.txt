### Front
How do you determine the direction of the magnetic field ($B$) at the centre of a circular current-carrying loop?

### Back
- Key idea: Use the Right-Hand Grip Rule (Right-Hand Rule for loops).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Curl right-hand fingers in the direction of the current ($I$); the thumb points in the direction of $B$.
- When to use it: Any time you need to find the magnetic field direction for a current-carrying loop or solenoid.
- Common trap: Using your left hand instead of your right hand, or confusing the thumb and finger roles.
