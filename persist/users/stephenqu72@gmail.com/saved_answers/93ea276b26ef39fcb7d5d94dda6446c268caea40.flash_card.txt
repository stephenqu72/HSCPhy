### Front
What observation did Edwin Hubble use to provide evidence for an expanding universe?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Galactic Red Shift (the observed increase in wavelength of light from distant galaxies).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Hubble’s Law: $v = H_0d$ (No fixed constant, $H_0$ is the Hubble constant).
- **When to use it:** When explaining the expansion of the universe or evidence for the Big Bang Theory.
- **Common trap:** Confusing Hubble's "Galactic Red Shift" (expansion) with "Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation" (the Big Bang afterglow discovered by Penzias and Wilson).
