### Answer
The process is the **proton-proton chain reaction**. It occurs in three main steps:

1.  Two protons ($^1H$) fuse to form a deuterium nucleus ($^2H$), releasing a positron ($^0_{+1}e$) and a neutrino ($\nu$).
    $$^1_1H + ^1_1H \rightarrow ^2_1H + ^0_{+1}e + \nu$$
2.  A proton ($^1H$) fuses with the deuterium nucleus ($^2H$) to form a helium-3 nucleus ($^3He$), releasing a gamma-ray photon ($\gamma$).
    $$^2_1H + ^1_1H \rightarrow ^3_2He + \gamma$$
3.  Two helium-3 nuclei ($^3He$) fuse to form a stable helium-4 nucleus ($^4He$), releasing two protons ($^1H$) to restart the cycle.
    $$^3_2He + ^3_2He \rightarrow ^4_2He + ^1_1H + ^1_1H$$
