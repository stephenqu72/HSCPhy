### Front
How do you calculate the resultant initial velocity ($u$) of a projectile given its horizontal ($u_x$) and vertical ($u_y$) components?

### Back
- Key idea: Vector addition of perpendicular components.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $u = \sqrt{u_x^2 + u_y^2}$ (Pythagorean theorem).
- When to use it: To determine the magnitude of the initial velocity vector from its component parts.
- Common trap: Forgetting that horizontal and vertical components must be calculated or treated independently before finding the resultant.
