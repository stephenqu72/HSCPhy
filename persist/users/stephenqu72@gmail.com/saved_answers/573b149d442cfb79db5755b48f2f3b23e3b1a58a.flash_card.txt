### Front
What are the three primary pillars of evidence supporting the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- Key idea: The universe is expanding from an initial hot, dense singularity.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When asked to justify the validity of the Big Bang model.
- Common trap: Forgetting that these evidences support an *expanding* universe rather than a static one. (1. Redshift, 2. Cosmic Microwave Background radiation, 3. Relative abundance of light elements).
