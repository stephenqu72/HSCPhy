### Front
What is the physical principle behind Millikan’s Oil Drop experiment, and what is the equilibrium condition used to calculate the charge of an oil droplet?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Charge quantization; charge exists only in integer multiples of the elementary charge ($e$).
- **Formula / law:** $F_E = F_g \implies qE = mg$
- **When to use it:** To determine the elementary charge $e$ or calculate the mass/charge of a suspended droplet in an electric field.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to convert units to SI (e.g., using grams instead of kilograms) or assuming the electric force $qE$ is the only force acting (must balance against weight $mg$).
