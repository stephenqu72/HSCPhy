To solve this problem, we need to calculate the magnetic flux through the coil and then account for the number of turns.

### 1. Identify the relevant formula
The magnetic flux ($\Phi$) through a single loop of area $A$ in a magnetic field $B$ is given by:
$$\Phi = B A \cos(\theta)$$
where $\theta$ is the angle between the **normal vector** (perpendicular to the face of the coil) and the magnetic field direction.

For a coil with $N$ turns, the total flux linkage is:
$$\Phi_{\text{total}} = N B A \cos(\theta)$$

### 2. Extract the given values
*   **Number of turns ($N$):** $11$
*   **Radius ($r$):** $10\text{ cm} = 0.1\text{ m}$
*   **Magnetic field ($B$):** $3 \times 10^{-5}\text{ T}$
*   **Area ($A$):** $\pi r^2 = \pi \times (0.1)^2 \approx 3.14 \times 10^{-2}\text{ m}^2$
*   **Angle ($\theta$):** The diagram shows the coil's axis is at $85^\circ$ to the North. The normal vector to the coil face is parallel to the axis of the coil. Since the magnetic field is directed towards the North, the angle between the field and the normal is $\theta = 85^\circ$.

### 3. Calculation
Substitute the values into the formula:
$$\Phi_{\text{total}} = 11 \times (3 \times 10^{-5}) \times (\pi \times 0.1^2) \times \cos(85^\circ)$$

Let's estimate:
*   $N \approx 10$
*   $B \approx 3 \times 10^{-5}$
*   $A \approx 3 \times 10^{-2}$
*   $\cos(85^\circ) \approx 0.087$

$$\Phi_{\text{total}} \approx 10 \times 3 \times 10^{-5} \times 3 \times 10^{-2} \times 0.087$$
$$\Phi_{\text{total}} \approx 9 \times 10^{-7} \times 0.087 \approx 7.8 \times 10^{-8}\text{ Wb}$$

Looking at the options, $10^{-7}\text{ Wb}$ is the closest order of magnitude. Even if we use precise values:
$$\Phi_{\text{total}} \approx 11 \times 3 \times 10^{-5} \times 0.0314 \times 0.0871 \approx 8.05 \times 10^{-7}\text{ Wb}$$

This value is very close to $10^{-6}\text{ Wb}$ (Option B) if we consider the calculation $10 \times 10^{-5} \times 10^{-2} \approx 10^{-6}$. However, because the angle is quite large ($85^\circ$), $\cos(85^\circ)$ is small ($\approx 0.087$), pulling the result down to the $10^{-7}$ range.

Comparing the result to the provided options:
**The correct answer is A.**
