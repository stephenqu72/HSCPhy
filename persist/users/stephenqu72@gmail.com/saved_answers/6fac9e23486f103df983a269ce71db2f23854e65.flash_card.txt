### Front
What is the general trajectory equation for a projectile launched from the origin $(0,0)$ to reach a point $(x, y)$?

### Back
- Key idea: Combining horizontal and vertical motion equations by eliminating time ($t$).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $y = x \tan(\theta) - \frac{gx^2}{2v^2 \cos^2(\theta)}$
- When to use it: When you need to find initial velocity ($v$) or launch angle ($\theta$) given a specific displacement $(x, y)$.
- Common trap: Forgetting to square the $\cos(\theta)$ term or mixing up units/angles (ensure calculator is in degrees).
