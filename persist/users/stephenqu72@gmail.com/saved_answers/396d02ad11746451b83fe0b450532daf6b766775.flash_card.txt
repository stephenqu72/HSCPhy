### Front
Why are Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites preferred for communication and imaging despite their short operational lifespans?

### Back
- Key idea: Reduced orbital altitude ($h$) minimizes signal travel distance.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When asked to justify the use of LEO satellites or compare them to Geostationary orbits.
- Common trap: Stating that "higher orbits are worse" without explicitly linking the *lower altitude* to *reduced latency* and *higher resolution*.
