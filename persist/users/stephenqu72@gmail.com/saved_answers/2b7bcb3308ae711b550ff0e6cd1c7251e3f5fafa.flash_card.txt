### Front
How do you determine the direction of the magnetic field produced by a solenoid?

### Back
- Key idea: The magnetic field direction inside a solenoid follows the direction of the thumb when using the Right-Hand Grip Rule.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Right-Hand Grip Rule (Fingers = Current $I$; Thumb = Magnetic Field $B$ inside).
- When to use it: Whenever you need to identify the poles of a solenoid or the direction of field lines at a specific point.
- Common trap: Confusing the direction inside the solenoid (S to N) with the direction of the external field lines (which loop back from N to S).
