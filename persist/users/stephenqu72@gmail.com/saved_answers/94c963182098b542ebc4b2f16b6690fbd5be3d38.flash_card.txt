### Front
How did James Chadwick experimentally determine the existence and properties of the neutron?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Bombarded beryllium with $\alpha$-particles to produce neutral radiation; then collided this radiation with paraffin wax (protons).
- **Formula / law:** Conservation of Momentum and Conservation of Energy.
- **When to use it:** When explaining the discovery of the neutron or evidence for subatomic particles.
- **Common trap:** Stating he used $E=mc^2$ to find the mass; he actually used kinematic collision analysis (conservation laws).
