3.  **Link to Nucleosynthesis:**

    *   Recall that lower-mass stars (like Y) have lower core temperatures, where the **proton-proton chain** is the primary mechanism for fusing hydrogen into helium.
4.  **Connect to Physical Characteristics:** Use the Mass-Luminosity relationship.
