### Front
What is the physical cause of "redshift" in astronomical observations?

### Back
- Key idea: The Doppler Effect causes light waves to stretch as an object moves away from the observer.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying the motion of distant galaxies or stars based on their spectral line shifts.
- Common trap: Confusing redshift (moving away, increased wavelength, decreased frequency) with blueshift (moving towards, decreased wavelength, increased frequency).
