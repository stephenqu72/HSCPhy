### Front
Why was the discovery of the neutron significantly delayed compared to other subatomic particles, and how was it finally detected?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Neutrons have no electric charge, meaning they do not ionize matter or deflect in electromagnetic fields, rendering them "invisible" to early detection methods (e.g., cloud chambers).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Conservation of momentum and energy applied to ejected protons.
- **When to use it:** When explaining the development of the nuclear model or experimental evidence for subatomic particles.
- **Common trap:** Confusing the discovery of the neutron with the discovery of the proton or nucleus; ensure you mention the use of paraffin wax for secondary detection.
