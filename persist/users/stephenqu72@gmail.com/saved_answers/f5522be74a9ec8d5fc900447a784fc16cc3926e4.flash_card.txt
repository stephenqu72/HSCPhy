### Front
In projectile motion (ignoring air resistance), what forces act on an object once it has left the launcher?

### Back
- Key idea: Only the gravitational force acts on the projectile; there are no horizontal forces.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $F_g = mg$ (acting vertically downwards); Newton’s First Law.
- When to use it: When analyzing 2D trajectories or free-fall motion in exam problems.
- Common trap: Including a horizontal force vector to "keep the object moving" horizontally.
