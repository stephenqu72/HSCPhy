### Front
What are the three rules for sketching the electric field lines between two like positive point charges?

### Back
- Key idea: Field lines originate from positive charges, repel each other, and cannot cross.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When sketching electrostatic field patterns for two positive charges.
- Common trap: Drawing lines that cross or failing to leave a "neutral point" (zero field strength) at the exact midpoint between the charges.
