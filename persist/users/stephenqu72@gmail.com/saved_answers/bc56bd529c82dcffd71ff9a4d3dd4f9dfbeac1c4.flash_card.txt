### Front
What is the standard procedure for determining the daughter nucleus in alpha decay?

### Back
- Key idea: Conservation of nucleon number ($A$) and atomic number ($Z$).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: ${}_{Z}^{A}P \rightarrow {}_{Z-2}^{A-4}D + {}_{2}^{4}\alpha$
- When to use it: Whenever a nucleus emits an alpha particle (Helium nucleus).
- Common trap: Forgetting to subtract 4 from the mass number or 2 from the atomic number of the parent nucleus.
