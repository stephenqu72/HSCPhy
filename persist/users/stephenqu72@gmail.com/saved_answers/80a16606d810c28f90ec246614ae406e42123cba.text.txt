### Explanation
Kepler’s Third Law states that for objects orbiting the same central body, the ratio of the cube of the orbital radius ($r^3$) to the square of the orbital period ($T^2$) is constant:
$$\frac{r^3}{T^2} = \frac{GM}{4\pi^2} = k$$
