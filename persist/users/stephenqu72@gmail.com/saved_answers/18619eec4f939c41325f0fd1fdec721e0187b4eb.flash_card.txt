### Front
What is the net force acting on an object moving in a straight line at a constant speed?

### Back
- Key idea: Equilibrium (zero acceleration).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Newton’s First Law ($\sum F = 0$).
- When to use it: Whenever a problem states "constant velocity," "steady speed," or "uniform motion."
- Common trap: Assuming that because an object is moving, there must be a net force acting on it.
