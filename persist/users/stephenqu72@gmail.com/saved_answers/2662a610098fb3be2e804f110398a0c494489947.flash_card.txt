### Front
What does the velocity-time graph for the vertical component of projectile motion look like, and why?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The graph is a straight line with a constant negative gradient.
- **Formula:** $v_y(t) = u_y - gt$
- **When to use it:** Whenever analyzing projectile motion under constant gravity.
- **Common trap:** Confusing the *displacement-time* graph (which is parabolic) with the *velocity-time* graph (which is linear).
