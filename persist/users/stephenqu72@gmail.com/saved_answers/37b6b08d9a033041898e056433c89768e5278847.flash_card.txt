### Front
What is the condition for a system (like an overhanging plank) to be on the verge of tipping?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Rotational equilibrium where the net torque about the pivot point is zero.
- **Formula / law:** $\sum \tau = 0$ (Clockwise torque = Counter-clockwise torque).
- **When to use it:** Problems involving static equilibrium, levers, or tipping thresholds.
- **Common trap:** Measuring the distance of the center of mass from the *wrong end* of the object rather than from the pivot point (fulcrum).
