### Front
What is Hubble’s Law and how does it relate to the expansion of the universe?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Galaxies move away from Earth at a velocity proportional to their distance; provides evidence for an expanding universe.
- **Formula / law:** $v = H_0 d$ (where $v$ = recession velocity, $H_0$ = Hubble constant, $d$ = distance).
- **When to use it:** When calculating the recession speed of distant galaxies based on their distance or interpreting redshift data.
- **Common trap:** Confusing redshift with Doppler shift of objects moving *through* space; Hubble expansion is the stretching of space itself.
