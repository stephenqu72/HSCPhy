Because they are more stable, they exist in a lower energy state. The "missing mass" (mass defect) is converted into kinetic energy and radiation, meaning the products weigh less than the reactants.

### How to solve:
1.  **Understand Stability:** A nuclear reaction is energetically favorable (exothermic/releases energy) if the products are more stable than the reactants.
2.  **Relate Stability to Binding Energy:** Higher total binding energy means the nucleus is more tightly bound and therefore more stable.
3.  **Relate Mass to Energy:** Using $E = \Delta mc^2$, a release of energy corresponds to a decrease in mass.
