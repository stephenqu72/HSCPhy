    $$F' = \frac{4 G m_1 m_2}{4 d^2}$$
5.  **Final calculation:** The factor of 4 in the numerator and the factor of 4 in the denominator cancel out:
    $$F' = \frac{G m_1 m_2}{d^2}$$
