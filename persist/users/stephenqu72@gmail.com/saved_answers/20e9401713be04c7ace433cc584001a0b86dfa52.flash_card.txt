### Front
What is the equation for the net force acting on a roller coaster cart at the top of a vertical loop?

### Back
- Key idea: Both gravity and the normal force act downward, providing the centripetal force.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $N + mg = \frac{mv^2}{r}$
- When to use it: Circular motion problems involving vertical loops at the highest point.
- Common trap: Forgetting that the normal force ($N$) is directed toward the center, not away from it, at the top of the loop.
