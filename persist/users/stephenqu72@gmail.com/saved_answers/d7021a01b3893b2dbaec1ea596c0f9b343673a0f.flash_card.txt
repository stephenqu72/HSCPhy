### Front
Distinguish between the processes of nuclear fission and nuclear fusion in a 2-mark HSC response.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Fission involves splitting a heavy nucleus into two smaller ones; Fusion involves combining two light nuclei into a heavier one.
- **Figure:** 
  - Fission: `1 Large Nucleus + 1 neutron → 2 Smaller Nuclei + 3 neutrons + Energy`
  - Fusion: `2 Small Nuclei → 1 Larger Nucleus + 1 neutron + Energy`
- **When to use it:** When asked to describe or contrast nuclear energy processes or transmutation.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to show the emission of neutrons; both processes must clearly indicate the release of energy and neutrons to satisfy marking criteria.
