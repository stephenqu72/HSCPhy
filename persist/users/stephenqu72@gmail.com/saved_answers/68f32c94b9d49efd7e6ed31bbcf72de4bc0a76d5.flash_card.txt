### Front
How did Edwin Hubble provide evidence for the expansion of the universe?

### Back
- Key idea: Distant galaxies exhibit a "cosmological redshift," meaning they are receding from Earth. The velocity of recession is directly proportional to their distance.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Hubble’s Law: $v = H_0 d$
- When to use it: When asked for evidence of the Big Bang or the expansion of space-time.
- Common trap: Thinking galaxies are moving *through* space like projectiles; in reality, space itself is expanding.
