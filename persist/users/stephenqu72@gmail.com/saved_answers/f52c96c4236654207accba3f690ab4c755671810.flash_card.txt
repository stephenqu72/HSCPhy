### Front
How do you determine the height or range of a projectile from a multi-exposure diagram in an HSC exam?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Use a scale factor to convert measured diagram distances into actual physical displacement.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $\text{Actual distance} = \text{Measured distance (cm)} \times \text{Scale factor (m/cm)}$.
- **When to use it:** When provided with a multi-exposure image containing a scale bar or a known reference height (e.g., a person).
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to measure from the **centre** of the object or failing to account for the initial release height when calculating range.
