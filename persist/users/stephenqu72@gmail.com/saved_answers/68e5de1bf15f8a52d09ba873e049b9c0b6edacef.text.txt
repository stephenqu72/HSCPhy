To find the magnetic force on a moving charge, we use the formula for the Lorentz force (magnetic component):

$$F = qvB\sin(\theta)$$

### 1. Identify the given values:
*   Charge of an electron ($q$): $1.6 \times 10^{-19}\text{ C}$
*   Velocity ($v$): $2.2 \times 10^6\text{ m/s}$
*   Magnetic field strength ($B$): $1.5\text{ T}$
*   Angle ($\theta$): Since the electron is fired into the page and the field is perpendicular to the motion, $\theta = 90^\circ$, so $\sin(90^\circ) = 1$.

### 2. Calculate the Magnitude:
$$F = (1.6 \times 10^{-19}) \times (2.2 \times 10^6) \times (1.5)$$
$$F = 5.28 \times 10^{-13}\text{ N}$$
