$$U = -\frac{GMm}{r}$$

$$r = R + h = 6.991 \times 10^7\text{ m} + 0.016 \times 10^7\text{ m} = 7.151 \times 10^7\text{ m}$$

$$U = -\frac{(6.674 \times 10^{-11}) \times (5.70 \times 10^{26}) \times 400}{7.151 \times 10^7}$$

$$(6.674 \times 10^{-11}) \times (5.70 \times 10^{26}) \times 400 \approx 1.52167 \times 10^{19}$$

$$U = -\frac{1.52167 \times 10^{19}}{7.151 \times 10^7} \approx -2.1279 \times 10^{11}\text{ J}$$
