The maximum kinetic energy ($E_k$) of an emitted electron is given by the photoelectric equation:
$$E_k = E_{\text{photon}} - \Phi$$

$$E_k = 5.0\text{ eV} - 4.3\text{ eV} = 0.7\text{ eV}$$
