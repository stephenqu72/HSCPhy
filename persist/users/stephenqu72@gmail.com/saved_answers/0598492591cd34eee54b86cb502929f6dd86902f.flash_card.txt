### Front
Classify the following particle categories: Hadrons, Baryons, Mesons, and Leptons.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Hadrons (made of quarks) divide into Baryons (3 quarks, fermions) and Mesons (quark-antiquark, bosons). Leptons are fundamental fermions (no quarks, not hadrons).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** To identify particle properties in Standard Model multiple-choice questions.
- **Common trap:** Assuming all fermions are hadrons; remember that leptons (like electrons) are fermions but are NOT hadrons because they don't contain quarks.
