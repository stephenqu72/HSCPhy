### Front
Why is the net work done on a satellite in a constant circular orbit equal to zero?

### Back
- Key idea: Total mechanical energy ($E = K + U$) remains constant because both orbital radius ($r$) and speed ($v$) are constant.
- Formula / law: Work-Energy Theorem ($W_{net} = \Delta E$).
- When to use it: To explain energy conservation in orbital motion or why centripetal force does no work.
- Common trap: Assuming that because the gravitational force is doing work, the net work must be non-zero; remember the force is always perpendicular to displacement.
