### Front
What is Hubble’s Law and what relationship does it describe between galaxies?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The universe is expanding; galaxies recede from Earth at a velocity proportional to their distance.
- **Formula:** $v = H_0 d$
- **When to use it:** To calculate the recessional velocity ($v$) or distance ($d$) of galaxies in cosmological expansion problems.
- **Common trap:** Confusing it with the Tully-Fisher relation (which relates rotational velocity to luminosity). Ensure you don't use it for local gravitationally bound objects.
