### Front
What is the total mechanical energy required for an object to reach escape velocity from a planet's surface?

### Back
- Key idea: Total mechanical energy must equal zero to allow an object to reach an infinite distance with zero kinetic energy.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Law of Conservation of Mechanical Energy ($K_i + U_i = 0$)
- When to use it: Whenever a question asks for the minimum velocity or energy required for an object to escape a gravitational field entirely.
- Common trap: Confusing escape velocity with the speed required to reach orbit; orbiting objects remain bound to the planet and possess negative total mechanical energy.
