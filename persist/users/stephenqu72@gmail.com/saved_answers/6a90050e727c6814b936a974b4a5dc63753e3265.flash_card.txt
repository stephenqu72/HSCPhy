### Front
How do you calculate the absolute uncertainty of a set of measurements for HSC Physics when using the range method?

### Back
- Key idea: Half the spread of the data.
- Formula / law: $\text{Uncertainty} = \frac{\text{Maximum} - \text{Minimum}}{2}$
- When to use it: When you have a small set of experimental trials and need to determine the precision of the mean.
- Common trap: Forgetting to divide the range by 2 or failing to round the mean to the same decimal place as the uncertainty.
