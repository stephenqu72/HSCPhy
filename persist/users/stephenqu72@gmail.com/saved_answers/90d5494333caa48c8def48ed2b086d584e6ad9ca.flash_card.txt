### Front
What is the core principle and method for solving 2D projectile motion problems?

### Back
- Key idea: Horizontal and vertical motions are independent and occur simultaneously.
- Formula / law / constant: Constant horizontal velocity ($v_x = \text{constant}$); vertical acceleration is gravity ($a_y = g = 9.8 \text{ m s}^{-2}$).
- When to use it: Any projectile motion problem where air resistance is neglected.
- Common trap: Forgetting that the velocity of impact is a vector (requires magnitude via Pythagoras and direction via $\tan^{-1}$).
