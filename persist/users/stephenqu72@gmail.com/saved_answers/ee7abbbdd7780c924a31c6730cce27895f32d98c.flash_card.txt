### Front
Outline the three key pieces of evidence supporting the Big Bang theory.

### Back
- Key idea: The universe originated from a hot, dense state and has been expanding ever since.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0d$) applies to the expansion aspect.
- When to use it: Any question asking for evidence for the Big Bang or the origin of the universe.
- Common trap: Merely listing the three terms (CMB, Redshift, Abundance) without explaining *how* they prove the theory; always link them back to an initial expansion/cooling event.
