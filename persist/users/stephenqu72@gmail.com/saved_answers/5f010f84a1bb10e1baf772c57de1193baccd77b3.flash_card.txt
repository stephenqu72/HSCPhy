### Front
Which exchange particle mediates the strong nuclear force that holds quarks together, and what is its classification?

### Back
- Key idea: The strong nuclear force is mediated by the **gluon**.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying Standard Model force carriers (gauge bosons) holding hadrons together.
- Common trap: Confusing gluons with mesons. Gluons are gauge bosons (force carriers), whereas mesons are hadrons made of a quark-antiquark pair.
