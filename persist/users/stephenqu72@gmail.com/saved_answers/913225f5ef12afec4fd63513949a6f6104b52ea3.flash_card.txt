### Front
Does the mass of an object projected into space affect its escape velocity? Explain why or why not.

### Back
- Key idea: Escape velocity is independent of the projectile's mass.
- Formula: $v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{r}}$
- When to use it: To calculate the minimum speed required to escape a gravitational field.
- Common trap: Assuming a heavier object requires more speed to escape; in reality, the mass ($m$) cancels out during the derivation of kinetic and gravitational potential energy.
