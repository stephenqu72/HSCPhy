### Front
Identify the six flavours of quarks and the gauge boson that mediates the force binding them within hadrons.

### Back
- Key idea: Quarks are classified into three generations (up/down, charm/strange, top/bottom); the strong nuclear force binds them.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: Any question involving the Standard Model, quark composition of hadrons, or strong interaction force carriers.
- Common trap: Confusing the gluon (strong force carrier) with $W$ or $Z$ bosons (weak force carriers).
