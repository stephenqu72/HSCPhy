### Front
What is the fundamental physical difference between a controlled and an uncontrolled nuclear chain reaction?

### Back
- Key idea: The management of the neutron population per fission event (neutron economy).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When explaining reactor dynamics (steady state) vs. nuclear weapons (exponential growth).
- Common trap: Assuming that the energy released per fission or the mass defect changes—these remain identical; only the reaction rate differs.
