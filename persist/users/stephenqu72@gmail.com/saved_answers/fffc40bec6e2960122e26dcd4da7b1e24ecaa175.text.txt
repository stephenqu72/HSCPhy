To solve this question, you need to recall the definitions of wave types based on the relationship between the direction of energy propagation (wave motion) and the direction of particle oscillation (vibrations):

1.  **Transverse Waves:** In these waves, the particles of the medium vibrate at right angles (perpendicularly) to the direction of energy transfer. An example of this is a wave on a string or electromagnetic radiation (like light).
2.  **Longitudinal Waves:** In these waves, the particles of the medium vibrate back and forth in the same direction (parallel) as the direction of energy transfer. An example of this is a sound wave traveling through air.
