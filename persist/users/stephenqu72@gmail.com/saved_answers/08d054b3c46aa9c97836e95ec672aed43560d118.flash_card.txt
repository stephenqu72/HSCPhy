### Front
How do you minimize measurement error when using a scale to record the projectile motion of an object?

### Back
- Key idea: Eliminate **parallax error** by ensuring the scale and the motion occur in the **same plane**.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: Whenever measuring displacement in a 2D plane (e.g., video analysis or lab experiments).
- Common trap: Placing a ruler on a surface (e.g., the ground) that is at a different depth to the object's path, causing the measured distance to shift based on the camera's line of sight.
