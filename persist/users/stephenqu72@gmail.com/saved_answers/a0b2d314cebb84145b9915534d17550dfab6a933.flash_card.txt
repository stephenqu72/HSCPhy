### Front
What is the fundamental physical difference between a controlled and an uncontrolled nuclear fission chain reaction?

### Back
- Key idea: The number of neutrons available to trigger subsequent fission events.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When comparing nuclear power reactors to atomic weapons.
- Common trap: Thinking the energy released per individual fission event differs; it is identical in both processes.
