### The Physics Principles
When an object is launched at an angle $\theta$, the initial velocity vector $v$ can be resolved into two perpendicular components:
1.  **Horizontal component ($v_x$):** $v_x = v \cos \theta$
2.  **Vertical component ($v_y$):** $v_y = v \sin \theta$
