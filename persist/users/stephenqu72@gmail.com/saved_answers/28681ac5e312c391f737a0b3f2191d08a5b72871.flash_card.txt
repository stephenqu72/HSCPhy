### Front
Outline the four fundamental forces of nature and their primary roles.

### Back
- **Key idea:** The four forces are:
    1. **Strong Nuclear:** Binds quarks/nucleons.
    2. **Electromagnetic:** Acts on charges (atoms/bonding).
    3. **Weak Nuclear:** Mediates beta decay/fusion.
    4. **Gravitational:** Acts on mass (large-scale structures).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** When asked to describe or compare the fundamental interactions governing matter and space-time.
- **Common trap:** Confusing the roles of the two nuclear forces (e.g., thinking the weak force holds the nucleus together instead of the strong force).
