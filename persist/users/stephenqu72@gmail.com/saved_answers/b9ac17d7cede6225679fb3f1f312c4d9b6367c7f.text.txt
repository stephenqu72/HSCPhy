### Answer
Two key concepts from Maxwell’s electromagnetic theory of light are:
1. **Light is an electromagnetic wave:** Maxwell proposed that light consists of oscillating electric and magnetic fields that propagate through space.
2. **Constant speed of light:** Maxwell’s equations predicted that the speed of these electromagnetic waves in a vacuum is a constant, given by $c = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0 \epsilon_0}}$, which matched the experimentally measured speed of light.
