### Front
How do you determine the polarity induced in a metal aircraft wing moving through the Earth’s magnetic field?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Motional EMF ($\varepsilon = Bv_\perp l$). Electrons experience a Lorentz force ($F = qvB$) and accumulate on one side of the wing, leaving the opposite side positively charged.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Right-Hand Rule (for positive charge) or Left-Hand Rule (for electrons).
- **When to use it:** When a conductor moves through an external magnetic field.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting that the force on electrons is opposite to the direction indicated by the Right-Hand Rule for positive charges.
