As an HSC Physics teacher, let's break this question down. This is a classic electromagnetism problem disguised as a magic trick.

### Analysis of the Physics
The question asks us to identify the graph representing the induced electromotive force (EMF) in a metal ring as it is thrown into the air. 

According to **Faraday's Law of Induction**, an EMF is induced in a conducting loop if there is a **change in magnetic flux** ($\Phi$) through the area enclosed by that loop:
$$\text{EMF} = -N \frac{\Delta\Phi}{\Delta t}$$

In this scenario:
1. The metal ring is in Earth's magnetic field.
2. As the magician throws the ring into the air, the ring undergoes projectile motion.
3. Crucially, the orientation of the ring relative to Earth's magnetic field **remains constant** as it moves through the air (assuming it doesn't tumble).
4. Because the magnetic field strength ($B$), the area of the ring ($A$), and the angle ($\theta$) between the field and the normal to the area are all constant during the flight, the magnetic flux $\Phi = BA \cos \theta$ remains **constant**.

Since the magnetic flux does not change ($\Delta\Phi = 0$), there is no induced EMF in the ring during its flight.

### Evaluating the Options
*   **Options A, B, and D** all suggest that there is a non-zero, time-varying, or constant EMF present, which would violate the law of conservation of energy in this context.
*   **Option C** shows an EMF of zero throughout the time interval. This correctly represents the physical situation where no change in magnetic flux occurs.

**Correct Answer: C**

### How to solve it
1.  **Identify the principle:** Recognise that this is an induction problem requiring Faraday's Law.
2.  **Determine the flux:** Ask, "Is the amount of magnetic field passing through the loop changing?" 
3.  **Evaluate variables:** Since the Earth's magnetic field is essentially uniform over the scale of the magic trick and the ring is not rotating or changing area, the flux is constant.
4.  **Conclude:** If flux is constant, the rate of change of flux is zero, therefore induced EMF must be zero. Look for the graph representing a constant zero value.
