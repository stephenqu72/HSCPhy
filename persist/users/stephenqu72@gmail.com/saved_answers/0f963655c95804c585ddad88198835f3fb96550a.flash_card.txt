### Front
What is the nature of the electric field between two parallel charged plates, and how is it represented on an $E$ vs $d$ graph?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The electric field is uniform (constant) between the plates.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $E = \frac{\sigma}{\epsilon_0}$ (represented as a horizontal straight line).
- **When to use it:** Whenever calculating field strength between two parallel plates with equal and opposite charge.
- **Common trap:** Assuming the field strength changes with distance ($d$) from one plate; it is constant everywhere between them.
