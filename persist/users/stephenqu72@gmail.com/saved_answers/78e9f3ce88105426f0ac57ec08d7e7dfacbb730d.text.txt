### Part (a): Calculate the length of the spring in Diagrams A and B

**Diagram A:**
The spring forms the horizontal leg of a right-angled triangle where the hypotenuse is the $25\text{ cm}$ section of the plank. Since the angle between the plank and the wall is $45^\circ$, the angle between the plank and the horizontal spring is $90^\circ - 45^\circ = 45^\circ$.
Using trigonometry:
$$L_A = 25 \times \sin(45^\circ) = 25 \times 0.707 \approx 17.68\text{ cm}$$

**Diagram B:**

$$L_B = 25\text{ cm}$$

*   At $L_B = 25\text{ cm}$: From the graph, $F_B \approx 36\text{ N}$.

**Torque Calculation ($\tau = F \cdot d \cdot \sin(\theta)$):**

    $$\tau_A = F_A \times 0.25\text{ m} \times \sin(45^\circ)$$
