2.  **Analyze the decay types:**
    *   **Alpha decay ($\alpha$):** Results in a loss of 4 in mass number ($A$) and 2 in atomic number ($Z$). This shows as a diagonal step down and to the left on the graph.
        *   $^{218}_{84}\text{Po} \rightarrow ^{214}_{82}\text{Pb} + ^{4}_{2}\text{He}$ (This is an **alpha decay**).

        *   $^{214}_{83}\text{Bi} \rightarrow ^{214}_{84}\text{Po} + ^{0}_{-1}e$ (This is a second **beta decay**).

3.  **Conclusion:**
    The sequence consists of one alpha decay (decreasing mass by 4 and atomic number by 2) followed by two beta decays (increasing atomic number by 1, twice, returning to the original atomic number of 84).

Therefore, the order is **Alpha decay, 2 beta decays**.
