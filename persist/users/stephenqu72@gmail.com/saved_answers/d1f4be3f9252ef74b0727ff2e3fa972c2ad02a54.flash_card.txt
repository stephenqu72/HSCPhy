### Front
Which fundamental law enabled Robert Millikan to determine the elementary charge in his oil drop experiment?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Force equilibrium ($F_{net} = 0$) allows for the calculation of charge by balancing electrical and gravitational forces.
- **Formula / law:** Newton's Second Law ($F_{net} = ma$); $qE = mg$
- **When to use it:** When an oil drop is held stationary or moving at a constant terminal velocity (acceleration is zero).
- **Common trap:** Confusing this with Newton's Third Law; equilibrium ($a=0$) is a direct consequence of the Second Law.
