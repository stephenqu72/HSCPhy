### Front
What was the primary purpose of Millikan’s Oil Drop Experiment?

### Back
- Key idea: To measure the elementary charge ($e$) of a single electron.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $F_e = F_g \implies qE = mg$; $e \approx 1.6 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$.
- When to use it: When balancing gravitational and electric forces on charged particles to determine individual charge values.
- Common trap: Mistaking the experiment as a method to determine the *mass* of an electron or the *sign* of the charge (which was already known).
