    *   **Polarisation** would require a polarizing filter (polaroid) to restrict the oscillations of the light wave.

Since the experiment specifically uses a single slit to observe how light behaves when forced through a narrow opening, it is the classic arrangement for demonstrating the wave nature of light, specifically **diffraction**.
