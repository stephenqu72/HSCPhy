### Front
What is the formula and physical principle used to calculate the escape velocity of a planet?

### Back
- Key idea: Total mechanical energy must be at least zero (kinetic energy compensates for gravitational potential energy).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}}$; Conservation of energy.
- When to use it: To determine the minimum initial velocity required for an object to escape a planet's gravitational field without further propulsion.
- Common trap: Attempting to include the mass of the escaping object in the calculation; escape velocity is independent of the object's mass.
