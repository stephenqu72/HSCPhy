### Front
How does mass affect the range of a projectile in ideal vs. real-world conditions?

### Back
- **Key idea:** In ideal conditions, range is independent of mass ($a=g$ for all). In reality, increased mass improves the ballistic coefficient, reducing the relative effect of air resistance.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Newton’s Second Law ($F=ma$); $a = g$ (ideal).
- **When to use it:** When asked about projectile design, shell modifications, or factors affecting trajectory.
- **Common trap:** Assuming mass affects the path in vacuum scenarios; always clarify whether air resistance is ignored.
