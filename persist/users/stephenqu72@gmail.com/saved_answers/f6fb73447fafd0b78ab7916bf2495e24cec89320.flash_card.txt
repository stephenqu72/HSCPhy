### Front
What are the three core pieces of observational evidence supporting the Big Bang Theory, and what does each prove?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The universe began as a hot, dense singularity and is currently expanding and cooling.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** 
    1. Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0d$): Proves expansion.
    2. Doppler Redshift: Proves galaxies are receding.
    3. CMB Radiation ($2.7\text{ K}$ blackbody curve): Proves the early, hot, opaque state.
- **When to use it:** For high-mark "evaluate" or "discuss" questions regarding the origin and evolution of the universe.
- **Common trap:** Confusing "Doppler redshift" (local movement) with "Cosmological redshift" (stretching of space). Also, failing to link CMB to the "recombination" phase where the universe became transparent.
