### Front
How do you determine the launch angle ($\theta$) of a projectile given its initial velocity components $v_x$ and $v_y$?

### Back
- Key idea: Use the inverse tangent of the velocity components to find the direction of the initial velocity vector.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\theta = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{v_y}{v_x}\right)$
- When to use it: To calculate the angle of projection when initial horizontal and vertical velocity components are known.
- Common trap: Forgetting to set your calculator to **degrees mode** instead of radians.
