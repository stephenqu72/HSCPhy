### Front
What evidence did Edwin Hubble use to conclude that the universe is expanding?

### Back
- Key idea: Distant galaxies exhibit a redshift in their light spectra, indicating they are receding from Earth.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0 d$).
- When to use it: When identifying empirical evidence for the expanding universe theory.
- Common trap: Confusing redshift (recession) with blueshift (approach, e.g., Andromeda galaxy) or the cosmological constant (originally used to maintain a static model).
