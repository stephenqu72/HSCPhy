### Front
What is the primary technological challenge in achieving controlled nuclear fusion for power generation?

### Back
- Key idea: Maintaining the extreme conditions (temperature, pressure, and confinement time) required to overcome the Coulomb barrier.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When asked about the feasibility or engineering difficulties of fusion reactors.
- Common trap: Confusing fusion with fission; fusion does not produce long-lived radioactive waste, but it is technically difficult to maintain the plasma state.
