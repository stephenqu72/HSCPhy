### Front
Which gauge bosons mediate the weak nuclear force?

### Back
- Key idea: The Standard Model defines exchange particles (gauge bosons) for fundamental forces.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying the particle responsible for radioactive decay processes (e.g., beta decay).
- Common trap: Confusing the W and Z bosons (weak force) with gluons (strong force) or photons (electromagnetic force).
