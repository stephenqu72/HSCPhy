### Front
How do you adjust a Cathode Ray Oscilloscope (CRO) to increase the displayed amplitude or the number of visible wave cycles?

### Back
- Key idea: Vertical sensitivity controls amplitude; Time base controls horizontal compression.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: To adjust waveforms that are too small (decrease V/div) or too "zoomed in" horizontally (increase s/div).
- Common trap: Confusing "sensitivity" settings; decreasing the V/div setting *increases* the visual amplitude on the screen.
