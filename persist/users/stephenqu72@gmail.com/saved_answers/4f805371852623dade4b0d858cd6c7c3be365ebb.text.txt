3.  **Lenz's Law:** To oppose the increase in magnetic flux (which is directed into the page), the induced current will create its own magnetic field directed **out of the page**. Using the right-hand grip rule, this induced current flows in a counter-clockwise direction.
4.  **Interaction with the Battery:** The battery is pushing current in a clockwise direction. The induced EMF acts in the opposite direction to the battery's EMF.
