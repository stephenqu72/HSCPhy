### Front
How do you calculate the change in gravitational potential energy ($\Delta U$) from a $U$ vs. $r$ (or altitude) graph?

### Back
- Key idea: Change is always the difference between the final and initial states.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\Delta U = U_{\text{final}} - U_{\text{initial}}$
- When to use it: Use graph data analysis to extract $U$ values at specific $r$ points, then subtract initial from final.
- Common trap: Incorrectly identifying the initial and final states; failing to account for the negative signs in gravitational potential energy calculations.
