### Front
What is the formula to calculate the banking angle ($\theta$) for a vehicle on a frictionless curved track?

### Back
- Key idea: The horizontal component of the normal force provides the centripetal force.
- Formula: $\tan(\theta) = \frac{v^2}{rg}$
- When to use it: When determining the ideal banking angle for a specific velocity ($v$) and radius ($r$) to avoid reliance on friction.
- Common trap: Forgetting to ensure units are SI (meters and m/s) or confusing $\tan(\theta)$ with $\sin(\theta)$.
