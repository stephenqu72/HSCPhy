### How to solve:
To solve nuclear cycle questions, always perform charge and nucleon conservation checks.
*   **Charge balance:** The sum of the subscripts (protons) on the left must equal the sum on the right.
*   **Mass balance:** The sum of the superscripts (nucleons) on the left must equal the sum on the right.
*   **Identify:** Once you determine the $A$ and $Z$ values, match them to the periodic table or standard nuclear particles (e.g., $^1H$ for proton, $^4He$ for alpha, $e^+$ for positron).
