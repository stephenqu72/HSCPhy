### Front
What quark transformation occurs during $\beta^+$ (positron) decay?

### Back
- Key idea: An up quark changes into a down quark ($u \rightarrow d$) via the weak nuclear force.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $p (uud) \rightarrow n (udd) + e^+ + \nu_e$
- When to use it: When asked to explain nuclear transmutation at the subatomic/Standard Model level.
- Common trap: Confusing $\beta^+$ decay (up to down) with $\beta^-$ decay (down to up).
