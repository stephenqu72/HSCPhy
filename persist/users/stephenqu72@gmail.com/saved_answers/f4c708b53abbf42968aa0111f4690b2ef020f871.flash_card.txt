### Front
What is Galileo’s principle regarding projectile motion?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The principle of **independence of perpendicular motion**; horizontal and vertical components are independent and occur simultaneously.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Horizontal: $a_x = 0$ ($v_x = \text{const}$); Vertical: $a_y = g$ (constant acceleration). Result: Parabolic trajectory.
- **When to use it:** When asked to explain, analyze, or draw a diagram of projectile motion.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to mention that the horizontal velocity remains constant (ignoring air resistance) or failing to show increasing vertical displacement in diagrams.
