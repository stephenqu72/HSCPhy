### Front
Outline an experiment to investigate the relationship between launch angle ($\theta$) and horizontal range ($R$) in projectile motion.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Range $R$ depends on $\theta$; the maximum horizontal range on level ground occurs at $45^\circ$.
- **Formula:** $R = \frac{v^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$
- **When to use it:** To determine the effect of launch angle on projectile distance while keeping launch velocity ($v$) and height constant.
- **Common trap:** Assuming the graph is linear; it is a sine wave peak at $45^\circ$. Ensure air resistance is minimized and trials are repeated to ensure reliability.
