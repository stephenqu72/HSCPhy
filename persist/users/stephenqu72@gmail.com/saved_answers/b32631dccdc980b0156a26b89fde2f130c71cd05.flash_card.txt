### Front
Define **reliability** in the context of an HSC Physics investigation and state the method used to ensure it.

### Back
- **Key idea:** Reliability refers to the **consistency** of data; obtaining similar results when repeating the experiment under the same conditions.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** When asked to evaluate the quality of your experimental data or justify your procedure.
- **Common trap:** Confusing reliability (consistency/repetition) with accuracy (closeness to true value) or validity (controlled variables/fair test).
