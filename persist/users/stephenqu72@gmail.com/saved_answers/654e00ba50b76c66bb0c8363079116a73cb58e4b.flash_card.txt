### Front
How do particle accelerators resolve the structure of subatomic matter and create new particles?

### Back
- Key idea: High momentum decreases de Broglie wavelength for resolution; kinetic energy converts to mass for particle production.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$ (de Broglie hypothesis) and $E = mc^2$ (Mass-Energy Equivalence).
- When to use it: To explain the physics behind high-energy collision experiments and the detection of subatomic structures.
- Common trap: Forgetting that acceleration is provided by electric fields, while magnetic fields are only used for steering and focusing.
