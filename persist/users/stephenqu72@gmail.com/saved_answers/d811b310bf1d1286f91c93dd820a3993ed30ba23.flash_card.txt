### Front
Why is the total mechanical energy of a satellite in an elliptical orbit conserved?

### Back
- Key idea: Gravity is a **conservative force**; work done is path-independent, meaning no energy is dissipated as heat.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $E_{total} = K + U = \text{constant}$
- When to use it: To explain orbital energy fluctuations where $K$ increases as $U$ decreases (moving toward perigee) and vice versa.
- Common trap: Forgetting to mention the absence of non-conservative forces (like atmospheric drag) that would otherwise remove energy from the system.
