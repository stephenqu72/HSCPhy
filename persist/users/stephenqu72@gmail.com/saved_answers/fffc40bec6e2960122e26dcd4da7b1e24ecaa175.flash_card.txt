### Front
Distinguish between transverse and longitudinal wave motion in terms of particle oscillation relative to energy propagation.

### Back
- Key idea: Transverse particles oscillate perpendicularly to energy flow; longitudinal particles oscillate parallel to energy flow.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When classifying wave types (e.g., sound vs. EM waves).
- Common trap: Swapping "perpendicular" (transverse) and "parallel" (longitudinal) definitions.
