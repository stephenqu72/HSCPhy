### Front
Why does the ISS experience a gradual decay in its orbital altitude over time?

### Back
- Key idea: Atmospheric drag causes a loss of orbital kinetic energy.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Newton’s Third Law of Motion.
- When to use it: Explaining orbital decay for satellites in Low Earth Orbit (LEO).
- Common trap: Attributing the altitude loss to the Moon's gravitational pull or simple mass loss rather than the force of residual air particles.
