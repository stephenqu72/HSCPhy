### Physics Principles
For an ideal transformer, the power input equals the power output ($P_{in} = P_{out}$). Since $P = VI$, this relationship is expressed as:
$$V_p I_p = V_s I_s$$
Which can be rearranged to show the inverse relationship between voltage and current:
$$\frac{V_p}{V_s} = \frac{I_s}{I_p}$$
