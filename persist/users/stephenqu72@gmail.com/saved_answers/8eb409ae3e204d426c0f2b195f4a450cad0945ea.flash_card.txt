### Front
What is the relationship between electric potential difference ($V$), uniform electric field intensity ($E$), and plate separation ($d$)?

### Back
- Key idea: In a uniform electric field, the field strength is the potential gradient between two parallel plates.
- Formula: $E = \frac{V}{d}$
- When to use it: Whenever calculating the electric field between parallel charged plates.
- Common trap: Failing to convert distance ($d$) from centimetres to metres (SI units).
