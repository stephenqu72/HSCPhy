### Front
What are the requirements for a balanced proton-proton chain nuclear fusion equation?

### Back
- Key idea: Conservation of nucleon number (mass), atomic number (charge), and lepton number.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $4_1^1\text{H} \rightarrow {}_2^4\text{He} + 2e^+ + 2\nu + \text{energy}(\gamma)$
- When to use it: To identify the net fusion process in main-sequence stars.
- Common trap: Forgetting the emission of two neutrinos ($\nu$) required to conserve lepton number during beta-plus decay.
