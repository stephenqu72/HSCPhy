### Answer
The device that contains an armature, a split-ring commutator, and brushes is a **DC motor** (or a DC generator).

**Function of components:**
*   **Armature:** A coil of wire wound around a soft iron core. When current flows through it within a magnetic field, the armature experiences a torque (in a motor) or has an induced EMF generated within it (in a generator).
*   **Split-ring commutator:** A ring split into two halves, insulated from each other. It acts as a mechanical switch that reverses the direction of the current in the armature every half-turn, ensuring the motor continues to rotate in the same direction.
*   **Brushes:** Usually made of carbon, these remain stationary and maintain electrical contact with the rotating commutator, allowing current to flow from the external circuit into the moving armature.
