Therefore, the **angle of refraction is greater**.
2.  **Snell's Law:** The relationship is given by:
    $$n_1 \sin(\theta_1) = n_2 \sin(\theta_2)$$

    $$\frac{n_1}{n_2} = \frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)}$$

4.  **Conclusion:** As the light moves from medium 1 to medium 2, the index of refraction **decreases**.
