### Front
Which of the following is NOT considered a primary observational pillar of the Big Bang Theory: Direct measurement of $H_0$, gravitational lensing, abundance of light elements, or the CMBR?

### Back
- Key idea: Gravitational lensing is a consequence of General Relativity (mass curving spacetime), not evidence for the universe's expansion or hot, dense origin.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When evaluating evidence for Big Bang cosmology (look for expansion, nucleosynthesis, and thermal afterglow).
- Common trap: Confusing evidence for General Relativity (like lensing) with evidence for the Big Bang Theory.
