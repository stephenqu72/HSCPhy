### Front
How did Planck resolve the "Ultraviolet Catastrophe" in black body radiation?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Classical physics incorrectly predicted infinite energy at high frequencies (UV). Planck proposed that energy is emitted/absorbed in discrete "quanta," meaning energy is quantized rather than continuous.
- **Formula / law:** $E = nhf$ (where $h$ is Planck's constant, $6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J}\cdot\text{s}$)
- **When to use it:** When explaining the shift from classical wave theory to quantum theory or the origins of the photon model.
- **Common trap:** Assuming energy is emitted continuously. Remember that $E=hf$ implies energy exchange only occurs in integer multiples of quanta.
