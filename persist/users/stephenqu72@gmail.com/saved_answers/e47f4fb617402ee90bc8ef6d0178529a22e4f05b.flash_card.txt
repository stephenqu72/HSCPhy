### Front
What is the formula to calculate the ideal banking angle ($\theta$) for a vehicle moving at velocity $v$ around a curve of radius $r$?

### Back
- **Key idea:** The horizontal component of the normal force provides the required centripetal force.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $\tan\theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$
- **When to use it:** When calculating the design angle of a banked track to prevent reliance on friction for circular motion.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to convert velocity from $\text{km/h}$ to $\text{m/s}$ before substituting into the formula.
