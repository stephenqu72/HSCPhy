### Front
What is the formula for the total mechanical energy ($E_{total}$) of a satellite of mass $m$ orbiting a planet of mass $M$ at distance $r$?

### Back
- Key idea: $E_{total}$ is the sum of kinetic ($K$) and gravitational potential ($U$) energy in a bound system.
- Formula: $E_{total} = -\frac{GMm}{2r}$
- When to use it: Calculating the energy required to change orbits or the total energy of a stable satellite.
- Common trap: Forgetting the negative sign; the negative sign indicates the satellite is in a bound state (it requires energy to escape to infinity).
