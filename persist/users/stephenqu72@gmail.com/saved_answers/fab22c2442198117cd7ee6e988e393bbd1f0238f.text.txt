**Suggested Fuel: Fusion (or Fission).**
Justification: Conventional chemical combustion is entirely impractical for interstellar travel because it requires a massive amount of fuel ($4 \times 10^8 \text{ kg}$), creating a "tyranny of the rocket equation" where the fuel itself adds too much mass to the craft. Fusion is often preferred over fission because it produces more energy per unit mass (if considering the total system) and leaves less radioactive waste compared to the heavy elements in fission products.
