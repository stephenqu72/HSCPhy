The fundamental principle governing the force on a charge in a magnetic field is the **Lorentz Force Law**:
$$F = qvB\sin(\theta)$$

2.  **The Scenario:** The problem describes a ball hung from a fixed point. If the ball is stationary, its velocity $v = 0$. Consequently, the magnetic force $F$ is $0$.
3.  **Evaluating the Diagram:** Because there is no force acting on the ball due to the magnetic field, the ball should hang vertically ($\theta = 0$).
