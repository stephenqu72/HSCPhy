### Front
How do you determine the location between two planets where a spacecraft experiences zero net gravitational force?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Set the gravitational force from Planet 1 equal to the force from Planet 2 ($F_1 = F_2$).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Newton’s Law of Universal Gravitation: $F = \frac{GMm}{r^2}$.
- **When to use it:** When solving for equilibrium points in a gravitational field between two masses.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to square root both sides of the equation after setting the forces equal.
