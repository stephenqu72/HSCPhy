### Front
How does a crumple zone reduce the average force experienced by passengers during a vehicle collision?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Increasing the duration of the impact ($\Delta t$) to decrease the magnitude of the average force ($F_{avg}$).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $F_{avg} \Delta t = m \Delta v$ (Impulse-Momentum Theorem)
- **When to use it:** Analyzing safety features, collisions, or any scenario involving impact mechanics.
- **Common trap:** Claiming momentum is "absorbed" (momentum is transferred/changed, not destroyed) or that speed is "absorbed."
