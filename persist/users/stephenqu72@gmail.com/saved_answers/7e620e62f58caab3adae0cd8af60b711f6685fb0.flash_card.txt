### Front
How do you determine the polarity of point charges using electric field lines?

### Back
- Key idea: Field lines originate from positive charges (source) and terminate on negative charges (sink).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Arrows point **away** from (+), **towards** (-).
- When to use it: Any diagram showing an electric field generated by point charges.
- Common trap: Confusing the direction of force on a test charge (which would move *with* the field line) with the movement of an electron (which would move *against* the field line).
