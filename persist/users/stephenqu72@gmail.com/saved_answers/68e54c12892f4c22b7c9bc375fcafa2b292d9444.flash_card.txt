### Front
What are the primary functions of moderators and control rods in a nuclear fission reactor?

### Back
- Key idea: Moderators thermalize (slow down) fast neutrons; control rods absorb excess neutrons to regulate the chain reaction rate.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When analyzing nuclear reactor safety, control systems, or the sustaining of chain reactions.
- Common trap: Confusing the two; remember that moderators *decrease* kinetic energy via elastic scattering, while control rods *remove* neutrons entirely via capture.
