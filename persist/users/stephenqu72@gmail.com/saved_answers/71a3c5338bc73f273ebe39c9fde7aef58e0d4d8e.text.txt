To solve this problem, we must understand the relationship between voltage ($V$), electric field strength ($E$), and distance ($d$) in a uniform electric field.

### Explanation

1.  **Identify the Relevant Physics Principle:**
    In a uniform electric field, the relationship between voltage, electric field strength, and the distance between plates is given by the formula:
    $$V = E \times d$$
    where:
    - $V$ is the voltage (in Volts)
    - $E$ is the electric field strength (in N/C or V/m)
    - $d$ is the distance between the plates (in meters)

2.  **Relate to the Graph:**
    The graph plots $V$ (y-axis) against $E$ (x-axis). According to the formula $V = d \times E$, this is in the form of a linear equation $y = mx$, where the slope $m$ represents the distance $d$.

3.  **Calculate the Slope ($d$):**
    We can choose two points on the line to calculate the slope $m = \frac{\Delta V}{\Delta E}$.
    Using the point at $E = 4\text{ N/C}$, $V = 0.20\text{ V}$ and the point at $E = 1\text{ N/C}$, $V = 0.05\text{ V}$:
    $$d = \text{slope} = \frac{0.20 - 0.05}{4 - 1}$$
    $$d = \frac{0.15}{3}$$
    $$d = 0.05\text{ m}$$

4.  **Convert to Appropriate Units:**
    The result $0.05\text{ m}$ is equivalent to $5\text{ cm}$ (since $1\text{ m} = 100\text{ cm}$, $0.05 \times 100 = 5$). Looking at the options, $5.00\text{ cm}$ matches option D.

### Answer
The correct answer is **D) 5.00 cm**.
