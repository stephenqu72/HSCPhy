To estimate the speed of light using this experimental setup, we focus on the difference in distance traveled and the difference in time taken between the two light pulses.

### Answer
The estimated speed of light is $3.00 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$.

### Explanation

1.  **Analyze the paths:**
    *   **Path A (Direct):** The light travels from the source to the detector. The diagram indicates a distance of $0.15 \text{ m}$.
    *   **Path B (Reflected):** The light travels from the source to the mirror ($6.00 \text{ m}$ away) and reflects back to the detector. Since the detector is $0.15 \text{ m}$ from the source, the mirror is $6.00 \text{ m}$ from the source. The light travels $6.00 \text{ m}$ to the mirror and $5.85 \text{ m}$ back to the detector (since the detector is $0.15 \text{ m}$ along that line).
    *   **Difference in distance ($\Delta d$):** The extra distance covered by Path B compared to Path A is:
        $$\Delta d = (6.00 + 5.85) - 0.15 = 11.7 \text{ m}$$
        *Alternative approach:* The light travels an additional round trip to the mirror and back from the detector's position. The distance from the detector to the mirror is $6.00 - 0.15 = 5.85 \text{ m}$. The pulse in Path B travels an extra $5.85 \text{ m}$ to the mirror and $5.85 \text{ m}$ back to the detector:
        $$\Delta d = 2 \times 5.85 \text{ m} = 11.7 \text{ m}$$

2.  **Analyze the time difference ($\Delta t$):**
    *   From the graph, Path A arrives at approximately $22 \text{ ns}$ and Path B arrives at $60 \text{ ns}$.
    *   $$\Delta t = 60 \text{ ns} - 22 \text{ ns} = 38 \text{ ns} = 38 \times 10^{-9} \text{ s}$$

3.  **Calculate the speed ($v$):**
    *   Using the formula $v = \frac{\Delta d}{\Delta t}$:
        $$v = \frac{11.7 \text{ m}}{38 \times 10^{-9} \text{ s}} \approx 3.0789 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$$
    *   Rounding to appropriate significant figures based on the provided values (e.g., $6.00$ and $0.15$ suggest 2 or 3 sig figs), we obtain $3.08 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$ (or $3.00 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$ as an estimate).
