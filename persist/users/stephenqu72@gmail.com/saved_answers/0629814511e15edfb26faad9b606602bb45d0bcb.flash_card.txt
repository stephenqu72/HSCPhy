### Front
What physical evidence demonstrates that the universe is expanding, and what is its governing relationship?

### Back
- Key idea: Galaxies exhibit redshift proportional to their distance from Earth, indicating recessional velocity.
- Formula / law: Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0d$)
- When to use it: When analyzing spectral line shifts in galaxies to determine their movement or distance.
- Common trap: Confusing Hubble’s Law (galactic expansion) with Bohr’s model (atomic line spectra) or the Doppler effect of sound (which does not involve the expansion of space itself).
