To solve these problems, we use the principles of conservation of momentum and Newton’s laws of motion.

### Question 6 Solution
**Concept:** Conservation of momentum. In any closed system where no external forces act, the total momentum before the collision equals the total momentum after the collision.

**Variables:**
- Mass of truck X ($m_X$) = $10\text{ tonnes}$
- Velocity of truck X ($u_X$) = $3.0\text{ m s}^{-1}$
- Mass of truck Y ($m_Y$) = ?
- Velocity of truck Y ($u_Y$) = $0\text{ m s}^{-1}$
- Final velocity of joined trucks ($v$) = $2.0\text{ m s}^{-1}$

**Calculation:**
$$p_{\text{initial}} = p_{\text{final}}$$
$$m_X u_X + m_Y u_Y = (m_X + m_Y)v$$
Substituting the values:
$$10 \times 3.0 + m_Y \times 0 = (10 + m_Y) \times 2.0$$
$$30 = 20 + 2m_Y$$
$$10 = 2m_Y$$
$$m_Y = 5\text{ tonnes}$$

**Answer:** The correct option is **B (5 tonnes)**.

### Question 7 Solution
**Concept:** Newton’s Third Law of Motion.
Newton’s Third Law states that for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction. When object X exerts a force on object Y ($F_{X \text{ on } Y}$), object Y simultaneously exerts a force of equal magnitude but opposite direction back on object X ($F_{Y \text{ on } X}$).

Mathematically, this is expressed as:
$$F_{X \text{ on } Y} = -F_{Y \text{ on } X}$$

**Answer:** The correct option is **C ($F_{X \text{ on } Y} = -F_{Y \text{ on } X}$)**.
