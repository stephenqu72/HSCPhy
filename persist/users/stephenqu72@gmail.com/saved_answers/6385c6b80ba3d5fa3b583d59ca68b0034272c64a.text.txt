### How to solve this type of question:
*   **Identify the poles:** Always note the direction of the magnetic field ($N \rightarrow S$).
*   **Trace the current:** Follow the wires from the battery to the coil to determine the direction of $I$ in the active sides of the coil.
*   **Apply the rule:** Always use the Left-Hand Rule for motors (remember: "Left" for "Logic/Motors", "Right" for "Generators").
*   **Verify symmetry:** Remember that in a standard motor, the forces on the two sides of the coil act in opposite directions to create rotation.
