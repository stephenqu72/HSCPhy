The atomic number increases by 1, transmuting the silicon into phosphorus ($^{31}_{15}\text{P}$).
    $$^{31}_{14}\text{Si} \rightarrow ^{31}_{15}\text{P} + ^{0}_{-1}\text{e} + \bar{\nu}_e$$
