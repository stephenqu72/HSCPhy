### Answer
The correct answer is **(C)**.

### Explanation
To solve this question, you need to understand the physical definition of **resonance**.

1.  **Define Resonance:** Resonance occurs in a physical system when it is driven by an external force at a frequency that matches its **natural frequency**. 
2.  **Condition for Resonance:** The mathematical condition for resonance is:
    $$f_{driving} = f_{natural}$$
    When the frequency of the external driving force ($f_{driving}$) is equal to the object's own natural frequency of vibration ($f_{natural}$), the system absorbs energy efficiently, resulting in maximum amplitude of oscillation.
3.  **Evaluate the Options:**
    *   (A) $30\text{ Hz} \neq 20\text{ Hz}$
    *   (B) $10\text{ Hz} \neq 40\text{ Hz}$
    *   (C) **$20\text{ Hz} = 20\text{ Hz}$** (This satisfies the condition for resonance)
    *   (D) $60\text{ Hz} \neq 30\text{ Hz}$

Because option (C) is the only pair where the driving frequency matches the natural frequency, it is the correct choice.
