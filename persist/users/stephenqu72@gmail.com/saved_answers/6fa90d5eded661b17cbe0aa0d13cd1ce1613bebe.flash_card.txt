### Back
- Key idea: Use a systematic method that explicitly addresses variables, reliability, and validity.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When asked to "design an experiment" or "describe a procedure" regarding projectile motion.
- Common trap: Stating "$45^{\circ}$" without describing a "fair test." You must mention repeats (reliability) and controlling launch velocity/height (validity) to gain full marks.
