### Front
Who first proposed the principle of relativity?

### Back
- Key idea: The laws of physics are the same for any system moving at a constant velocity (Galilean Relativity).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure
- When to use it: Identifying the historical foundation of classical relativity.
- Common trap: Attributing the origin of the principle to Einstein, who actually expanded it to include electromagnetism and light.
