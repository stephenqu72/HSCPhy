### Front
What formula is used to calculate the range of a projectile launched and landing at the same vertical height?

### Back
- Key idea: Horizontal range depends on initial velocity, launch angle, and gravity.
- Formula: $R = \frac{v^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$
- When to use it: When the object lands at the same vertical level as the launch point (Level Ground).
- Common trap: Forgetting to double the angle inside the sine function ($\sin(2\theta)$) or using it when the landing height is different from the launch height.
