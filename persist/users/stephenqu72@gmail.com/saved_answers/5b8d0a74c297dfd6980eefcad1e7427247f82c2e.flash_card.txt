### Front
What is the primary technical barrier to achieving controlled nuclear fusion for commercial power generation?

### Back
- Key idea: The **Coulomb barrier** (electrostatic repulsion between positively charged nuclei).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When comparing the engineering challenges of nuclear fusion vs. nuclear fission.
- Common trap: Assuming fusion is impractical due to radioactive waste or fuel scarcity; the real issue is the extreme heat/pressure required to force nuclei together.
