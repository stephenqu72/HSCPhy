### Front
What is the physical principle underlying the energy release in nuclear fission?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Mass-energy equivalence; the "mass defect" ($\Delta m$) between reactants and products is converted into kinetic energy.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** $E = \Delta m c^2$
- **When to use it:** To calculate energy release when the total mass of products is less than the total mass of reactants.
- **Common trap:** Assuming mass is conserved (mass is *not* conserved in nuclear reactions; it is transformed into energy).
