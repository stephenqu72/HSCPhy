### Front
What is the expression for the charge $Q$ of an oil drop balanced between two parallel plates in the Millikan experiment?

### Back
- Key idea: Equilibrium between gravitational force ($mg$) and upward electric force ($qE$).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $Q = \frac{mgd}{V}$
- When to use it: When an oil drop is stationary (net force = 0) in a uniform electric field.
- Common trap: Forgetting to account for the buoyant force if asked for the *actual* mass (though standard HSC questions assume $F_g = F_e$).
