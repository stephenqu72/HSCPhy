### Front
What is the structural blueprint for achieving full marks in a 7-mark HSC Physics practical investigation response?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Explicitly address the five mandatory components: Hypothesis, Variables (Ind/Dep/Ctrl), Method, Results, and Conclusion.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** Any "design an investigation" or "evaluate an experiment" question worth 5+ marks.
- **Common trap:** Failing to explicitly link the experimental data back to the theoretical formula (e.g., $F_c = \frac{mv^2}{r}$) or neglecting to define controlled variables, which invalidates reliability.
