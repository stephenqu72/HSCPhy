### Front
What evidence did Edwin Hubble use to conclude that the Universe is expanding?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Galaxies exhibit a systematic "redshift" in their spectral lines, indicating they are receding from Earth.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** Hubble's Law ($v = H_0d$)
- **When to use it:** To explain the observational basis for the Big Bang theory and the expansion of space.
- **Common trap:** Confusing cosmological redshift with the Doppler effect of light; while similar, cosmological redshift is caused by the stretching of space itself, not just the motion of galaxies through space.
