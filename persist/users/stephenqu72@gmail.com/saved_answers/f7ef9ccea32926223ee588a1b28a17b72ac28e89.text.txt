    *   Generation 1: **up** and **down**
    *   Generation 2: **charm** and **strange**
    *   Generation 3: **top** and **bottom**

2.  **Determine the main force carrier:** Quarks possess a property called "colour charge." The fundamental force that acts between particles with colour charge is the **strong nuclear force**. 
    *   The gauge boson responsible for mediating the strong nuclear force is the **gluon**.
    *   The $W$ and $Z$ bosons are the force carriers for the *weak* nuclear force, not the primary force keeping quarks together in hadrons.

3.  **Conclusion:** Since rows C and D are the only ones with the correct list of quarks, and the **gluon** is the force carrier for the strong interaction (which binds quarks), option **D** is the correct choice.
