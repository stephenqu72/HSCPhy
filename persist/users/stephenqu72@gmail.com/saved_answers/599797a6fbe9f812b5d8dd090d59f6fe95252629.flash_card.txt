### Front
What are the two primary pieces of electromagnetic radiation evidence supporting the Big Bang theory?

### Back
- Key idea: The universe is expanding (Hubble's Law) and retains the "afterglow" of its hot, dense origin (CMB).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $v = H_0d$ (Hubble’s Law); CMB represents a blackbody temperature of $\approx 2.7\text{ K}$.
- When to use it: Any question asking for empirical evidence of the Big Bang or the evolution of the universe.
- Common trap: Confusing "redshift" with a Doppler effect caused by galaxies moving *through* space; specify that space itself is expanding.
