### Front
How should you structure an HSC Physics "9-mark" response regarding experimental design and evaluation?

### Back
- Key idea: Structure by defining variables (IV/DV/Controls), justifying controls for **validity**, and evaluating limitations for **reliability**.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: Any extended response question asking to design, justify, or evaluate an experiment (e.g., Pendulum, Snell's Law, Hooke's Law).
- Common trap: Neglecting to explicitly link the control of variables to the specific scientific principles being tested (e.g., Simple Harmonic Motion limits).
