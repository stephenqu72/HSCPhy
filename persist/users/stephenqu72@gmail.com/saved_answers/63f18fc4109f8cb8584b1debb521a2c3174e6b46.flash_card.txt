### Front
What experimental term is evaluated by repeating an investigation using the same methodology and conditions?

### Back
- Key idea: Reliability (the consistency of experimental results).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When checking if an experimental procedure produces consistent, reproducible data.
- Common trap: Confusing **reliability** (consistency/repetition) with **validity** (correct experimental design/controlling variables) or **accuracy** (proximity to the true/accepted value).
