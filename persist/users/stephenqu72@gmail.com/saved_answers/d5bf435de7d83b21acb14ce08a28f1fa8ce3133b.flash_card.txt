### Front
How should you approach a "Select, Outline, and Assess" question regarding evidence for the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- **Key idea:** Structure your response to address all three verbs: name the evidence, explain the physics, and make a value judgment.
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** When asked to evaluate scientific evidence (e.g., CMB or Hubble’s Law) to support a cosmological model.
- **Common trap:** Forgetting to "Assess"—stating what the evidence is (Outline) without explicitly judging its strength or validity in supporting the theory.
