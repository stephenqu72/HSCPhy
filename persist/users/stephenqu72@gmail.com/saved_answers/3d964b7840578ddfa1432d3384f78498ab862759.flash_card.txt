How do you balance a nuclear fission equation?

### Back
- Key idea: Conservation of nucleon number (mass) and atomic number (charge).
- Formula / law: $\sum A_{initial} = \sum A_{final}$ and $\sum Z_{initial} = \sum Z_{final}$.
- When to use it: To identify unknown isotopes or particles in nuclear decay/fission reactions.
- Common trap: Forgetting to multiply the subscript/superscript by the coefficient (e.g., the $3$ in $3({}^{1}_{0}n)$).
