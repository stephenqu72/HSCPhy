$$\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}}$$

2.  **Square the ratio:**

    $$1 - 0.485809 = 0.514191$$
    $$\sqrt{0.514191} \approx 0.717071$$
4.  **Calculate $\gamma$:**
    $$\gamma = \frac{1}{0.717071} \approx 1.39456$$
