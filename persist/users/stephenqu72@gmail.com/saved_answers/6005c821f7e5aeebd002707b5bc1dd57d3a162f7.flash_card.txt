### Front
What are the two primary pieces of evidence supporting the Big Bang Theory?

### Back
- Key idea: Evidence of an expanding, cooling universe originating from a singularity.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Hubble’s Law ($v = H_0d$) and Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) radiation.
- When to use it: When asked to justify the Big Bang model or the evolution of the universe.
- Common trap: Confusing stellar classification (H-R diagrams) or orbital mechanics (Kepler’s laws) with cosmological origins.
