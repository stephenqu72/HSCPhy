### Front
When analyzing a video analysis of projectile motion, what experimental error occurs if the camera is not perpendicular to the plane of motion?

### Back
- Key idea: Foreshortening of the trajectory.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: To explain systematic errors in displacement measurement during 2D motion analysis.
- Common trap: Confusing "foreshortening" (camera angle) with "parallax error" (ruler depth/alignment).
