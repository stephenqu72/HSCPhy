### Front
How do you determine the separation distance ($d$) between two parallel plates from a $V$ vs $E$ graph?

### Back
- Key idea: The slope of a $V$ vs $E$ graph equals the distance ($d$).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $V = Ed$ (where slope $m = d$).
- When to use it: Whenever given a linear plot of Voltage against Electric Field strength.
- Common trap: Forgetting to convert units (e.g., meters to centimeters) at the final step.
