### Front
What observation by Edwin Hubble provided the first empirical evidence for the Big Bang Theory, and what is the associated mathematical relationship?

### Back
- Key idea: Galactic red shift indicates the universe is expanding; galaxies recede at a speed proportional to their distance.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: Hubble’s Law: $v = H_0 d$ ($H_0$ is the Hubble constant).
- When to use it: To explain the expansion of the universe or calculate the recession velocity of a distant galaxy.
- Common trap: Confusing Hubble’s discovery (red shift/expansion) with the Cosmic Microwave Background (Penzias and Wilson).
