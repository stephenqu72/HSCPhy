### Front
What does the acceleration-time graph look like for a projectile in flight (ignoring air resistance)?

### Back
- Key idea: Acceleration is constant and directed downwards throughout the entire flight.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $a = g \approx 9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$ downwards.
- When to use it: Any projectile motion problem to determine the acceleration at any point (including the peak).
- Common trap: Assuming acceleration is zero at the maximum height because velocity is zero.
