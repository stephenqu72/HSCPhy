$$F = BIL \sin(\theta)$$

*   **Magnetic field strength ($B$):** $0.50\text{ T}$

*   **Angle ($\theta$):** $45^\circ$

### Step 2: Substitute the values into the formula
$$F = (0.50) \times (0.040) \times (0.120) \times \sin(45^\circ)$$

### Step 3: Calculate the result

    $$0.50 \times 0.040 \times 0.120 = 0.0024$$
2.  Calculate $\sin(45^\circ) \approx 0.7071$
3.  Combine them:
    $$F = 0.0024 \times 0.7071 \approx 0.001697\text{ N}$$
