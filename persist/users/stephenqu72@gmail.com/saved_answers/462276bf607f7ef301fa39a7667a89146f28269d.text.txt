### Answer
The correct answer is **(B) into the page**.

---

### Explanation
To determine the direction of the magnetic field created by a long straight current-carrying wire, we use the **Right-Hand Grip Rule** (sometimes called the Right-Hand Thumb Rule).

1.  **Identify the orientation:** Point your right thumb in the direction of the conventional current $I$ (to the right, as shown by the arrow on the wire).
2.  **Determine the curl:** Curl your fingers around the wire. Your fingers represent the circular path of the magnetic field lines.
3.  **Analyze point P:**
    *   Above the wire, your fingers would be pointing *out* of the page.
    *   Below the wire, where point $P$ is located, your fingers are pointing *into* the page.

Since the point $P$ is situated below the wire, the magnetic field lines pass through the plane of the page in a direction going **into the page**.
