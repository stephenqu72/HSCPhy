### Front
What was the primary objective and the fundamental principle determined by Millikan’s Oil Drop experiment?

### Back
- Key idea: Determined the elementary charge of an electron and proved that electric charge is quantized.
- Formula / law / constant: $qE = mg$; $e \approx 1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C.
- When to use it: When balancing electric and gravitational forces on a charged particle to find its charge ($q$).
- Common trap: Confusing Millikan’s experiment with J.J. Thomson’s work, which measured the *charge-to-mass ratio* ($q/m$), not the individual charge.
