### Front
What is the order of penetrating power for ionizing radiation ($\alpha, \beta, \gamma$) and their respective shielding requirements?

### Back
- Key idea: Penetrating power is inversely proportional to mass, charge, and ionizing ability.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $\alpha$ (stopped by paper) < $\beta$ (stopped by Al) < $\gamma$ (stopped by thick lead/concrete).
- When to use it: To identify unknown radiation sources based on their ability to pass through various materials in experimental shielding setups.
- Common trap: Confusing ionizing power with penetrating power; $\alpha$ has the *highest* ionizing power but the *lowest* penetration.
