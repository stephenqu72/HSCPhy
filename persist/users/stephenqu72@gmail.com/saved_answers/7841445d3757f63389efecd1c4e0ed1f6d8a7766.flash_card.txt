### Front
How do you calculate the force required to maintain a limb in static rotational equilibrium?

### Back
- Key idea: Principle of Moments (Torque).
- Formula / law: $\sum \tau_{\text{clockwise}} = \sum \tau_{\text{counter-clockwise}}$ where $\tau = Fd\sin\theta$.
- When to use it: When an object is stationary and acted upon by multiple forces at varying distances from a pivot.
- Common trap: Failing to measure all distances from the **same** pivot point or forgetting to convert all units to be consistent (e.g., mm to m).
