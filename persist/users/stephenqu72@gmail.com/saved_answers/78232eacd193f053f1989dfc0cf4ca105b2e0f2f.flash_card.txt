### Front
Which gauge bosons mediate the Weak Nuclear Force in the Standard Model?

### Back
- Key idea: The Weak Nuclear Force is carried by the $W^+, W^-,$ and $Z^0$ bosons.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying exchange particles (gauge bosons) for fundamental forces, specifically processes like beta decay.
- Common trap: Confusing $W$ and $Z$ bosons with the Higgs boson (which gives mass) or photons (which carry electromagnetism).
