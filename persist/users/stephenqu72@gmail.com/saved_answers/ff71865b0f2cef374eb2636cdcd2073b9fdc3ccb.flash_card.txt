### Front
In an experiment investigating circular motion using a rotating disc with holes at varying distances, what is the independent variable?

### Back
- Key idea: The variable intentionally manipulated by the experimenter.
- Formula / law / constant / figure: No fixed constant or figure.
- When to use it: When identifying experimental design components (IV, DV, CV) in circular motion practicals.
- Common trap: Confusing the independent variable (radius/peg position) with the dependent variable (resulting linear velocity).
