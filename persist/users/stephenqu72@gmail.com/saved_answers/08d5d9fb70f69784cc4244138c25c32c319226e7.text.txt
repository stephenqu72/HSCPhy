### Explanation

2.  **Applying the rule to the diagram:**
    *   The current is flowing upward along the straight wire.
    *   Point your right thumb upward along the line of the wire.
    *   Your fingers curl around the wire in a counter-clockwise direction (when looking at the wire from above).
    *   This means that on the right side of the wire, the field points into the page, and on the left side of the wire, the field points out of the page. This creates a circular field pattern around the wire.
3.  **Evaluating the options:**

    *   **Option C:** Shows a field pattern similar to that of a bar magnet or a current loop, not a straight wire.
