### Front
Outline the four fundamental forces of nature.

### Back
- **Key idea:** The four forces are Gravity (long-range, mass-based), Electromagnetism (charge-based, atomic/molecular structure), Strong Nuclear (short-range, binds nuclei), and Weak Nuclear (short-range, beta decay/fusion).
- **Formula / law / constant / figure:** No fixed constant or figure.
- **When to use it:** To describe universal interactions or the structure of matter within the Standard Model.
- **Common trap:** Confusing the roles of the Strong Nuclear force (holding the nucleus together) with the Weak Nuclear force (radioactive decay).
