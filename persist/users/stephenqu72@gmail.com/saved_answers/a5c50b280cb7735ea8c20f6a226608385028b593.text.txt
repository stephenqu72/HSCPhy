$$\frac{mv^2}{r} = \frac{GMm}{r^2}$$

$$v = \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}$$

From this formula, we can see that the orbital velocity $v$ is inversely proportional to the square root of the orbital radius $r$:
$$v \propto \frac{1}{\sqrt{r}}$$
