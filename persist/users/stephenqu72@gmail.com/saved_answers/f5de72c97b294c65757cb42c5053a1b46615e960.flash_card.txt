### Front
Describe the electric field between two parallel charged plates and how it changes with distance $x$ between them.

### Back
- Key idea: The electric field is uniform (constant in magnitude and direction).
- Formula / law / constant / figure: $E = \frac{V}{d}$; Graph is a horizontal line ($E$ vs $x$).
- When to use it: To determine field strength or particle acceleration between capacitor plates.
- Common trap: Assuming the electric field varies with distance $x$ from one plate; it is independent of position $x$.
