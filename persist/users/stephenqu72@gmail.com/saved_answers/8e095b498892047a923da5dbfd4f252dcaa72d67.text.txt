To determine the correct graph, we must apply Faraday’s Law of Induction, which states that the induced electromotive force (voltage) is proportional to the negative rate of change of magnetic flux:
$$\varepsilon = - \frac{d\Phi}{dt}$$

    In the diagram, the plane of the coil is parallel to the magnetic field lines.
